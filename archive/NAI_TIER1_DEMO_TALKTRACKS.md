# NAI Tier 1 — Demo Talk Tracks (2–3 minutes each)

Use these for live demos. Short, clear, focused on outcomes.

---

## 1) Ducro Funeral Services — Compassionate Planning
**Hook:** “Families need a quiet way to start hard conversations.”
- This tool guides pre‑planning without pressure.
- Families choose traditional/cremation/memorial/green options.
- AI generates a gentle summary they can review at home.
- Ducro stays the trusted guide; the tool just reduces friction.
**Outcome:** Fewer cold calls, more prepared families, less admin time.

---

## 2) Maritime Museum — Pocket Historian Pro
**Hook:** “History comes alive when it talks back.”
- GPS‑aware tours with historical characters.
- Visitors pick a narrator (lighthouse keeper, dock worker, captain).
- Instant story + CTA to explore more.
**Outcome:** Longer dwell time, more repeat visits, easier ticket upsell.

---

## 3) Maritime Museum — AI Docent
**Hook:** “Point your phone at an artifact — get a narration in seconds.”
- Camera capture or upload.
- AI generates museum‑quality narration + audio playback.
- Works for special exhibits and temporary displays.
**Outcome:** Scales docent coverage without losing quality.

---

## 4) Ferrante Winery — Sommelier Pro
**Hook:** “Every table gets a sommelier.”
- QR code at tables → guests scan.
- AI pairing recommendations + premium upsell.
- Staff gets a backup for busy nights.
**Outcome:** Higher ticket size, consistent upsells, faster service.

---

## 5) Ashtabula Harbor Cam
**Hook:** “Real‑time transparency for safety + tourism.”
- Live camera views of harbor, lighthouse, bridge.
- Marine conditions + forecast.
- Public‑facing safety dashboard.
**Outcome:** Trust, safety awareness, tourism engagement.

---

## 6) Ashtabula Business Financial (Cashflow)
**Hook:** “Small businesses need fast, clear cashflow guidance.”
- Weekly income/expense snapshot.
- Instant AI insights + trend chart.
- Plain‑language recommendations.
**Outcome:** Better decisions without hiring a CFO.

---

## 7) Chamber Contractor Match
**Hook:** “The Chamber becomes the trusted connector.”
- Homeowners enter trade → vetted contractor match.
- Chamber endorsement baked in.
- Leads routed to members.
**Outcome:** Member value + community trust.

---

**Tip:** Close every demo with a simple question: “Would you like a pilot version for your organization?”
