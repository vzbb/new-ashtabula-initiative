## Pricing Strategy

### Freemium Model (Most Tools)
| Tier | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Free** | $0 | Core functionality, limited usage |
| **Pro** | $9-29/mo | Unlimited usage, advanced features |
| **Business** | $49-99/mo | Multi-user, API access, priority support |

### SaaS Examples
- **service-scheduler:** Free → Pro $9/mo → Business $19/mo
- **grantgenius:** Free → Pro $29/mo → Network $79/mo
- **cashflow-tracker:** Free → Pro $19/mo → Business $49/mo

### Civic Tools (Free)
Most civic/government tools offered at no cost to:
- City of Ashtabula
- Ashtabula County
- Nonprofit organizations
- Community groups

## Sales Approach

### Method: Consultative + Demonstration
1. **Research** - Understand prospect's pain points
2. **Demonstrate** - Show working prototype
3. **Pilot** - Free trial period
4. **Customize** - Adapt to specific needs
5. **Convert** - Subscription or custom build

### Pitch Structure
1. **Problem** - "You mentioned [specific pain point]..."
2. **Solution** - "We built [tool] specifically for this..."
3. **Demo** - Live working prototype
4. **Social Proof** - Other local users/adoption
5. **Pricing** - Freemium/low-cost model
6. **Next Steps** - Pilot or implementation timeline
