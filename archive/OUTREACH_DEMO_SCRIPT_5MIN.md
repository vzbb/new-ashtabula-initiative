# 5‑Minute Demo Script (Universal)

**Goal:** Quick, consistent demo regardless of tool. Adjust details per site.

---

## 1) Opening (30 seconds)
"Thanks for taking a few minutes. I’m Rondell Williams. I build practical AI tools for Ashtabula County organizations — not big, complicated systems, just small tools that solve real problems fast. I’d like to show you one in under five minutes and get your honest reaction."

---

## 2) Context (30 seconds)
"This tool is built for *your* world. It’s not generic software — it’s local, branded, and tuned to your workflow. It runs in a browser, so your team can use it immediately without training or downloads."

---

## 3) Live Walkthrough (2–3 minutes)
**Steps:**
1. Enter a real input (example: dish name, contractor type, artifact photo).
2. Show the AI result in seconds.
3. Highlight one outcome (less admin, higher upsell, better visitor engagement).

---

## 4) Outcome (30 seconds)
"The goal is simple: reduce friction, save time, and create a better experience for the people you serve. It’s an add‑on, not a replacement."

---

## 5) Close (30 seconds)
"If this feels useful, we can pilot it with your team and adjust from there. If not, no worries — I appreciate your time. Would a pilot make sense for you?"

---

**Tip:** Always end with a *yes/no* question.
