# Noirsys AI Outreach Playbook

**Purpose:** Systematic approach to engaging prospects and partners  
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-15

---

## Outreach Channels

### 1. Email Campaigns
- **Cold outreach** to identified prospects
- **Follow-up sequences** after initial contact
- **Newsletter** for engaged contacts

### 2. In-Person Meetings
- **Chamber of Commerce** events
- **SBDC workshops** and office hours
- **Direct office visits** to priority prospects

### 3. Digital Presence
- **LinkedIn** professional networking
- **Facebook groups** (community engagement)
- **Local media** (Star Beacon, community radio)

### 4. Partnership Leverage
- **Referrals** from SBDC, Chamber, Growth Partnership
- **Endorsements** from City/County officials
- **Case studies** from early adopters

## Outreach Templates

### Template 1: Business Cold Email

**Subject:** Free tool for [specific problem] - built for Ashtabula

```
Hi [Name],

I noticed [business name] [observation about their current process/pain point].

We built [tool name] specifically to help Ashtabula businesses with [problem].
It's a working prototype - not a pitch deck - and we'd love to show you.

**What it does:**
- [Key feature 1]
- [Key feature 2]
- [Key feature 3]

**The catch:** There isn't one. It's free during our pilot phase.
We're looking for 3-5 local businesses to test it and give feedback.

**Next step:** 15-minute demo at your convenience?

Best,
[Your name]
Noirsys AI
[Phone] | [Email]
```

### Template 2: Government Partnership

**Subject:** Partnership opportunity - modernizing [service] for residents

```
[Name],

I'm reaching out about a civic tech project we're building that aligns with 
[City/Department]'s goals around [specific initiative].

We developed [tool name] - a [description] that helps residents [benefit].
Think of it as [simple analogy].

**Why partner with us:**
- Zero cost to the City
- We handle all development and maintenance
- Modern service without IT overhead
- You get credit for improved resident experience

**Similar success:** [Reference if available]

Could we schedule 20 minutes to show you the working prototype?

[Your name]
Noirsys AI
```

### Template 3: Nonprofit Collaboration

**Subject:** Free tool for [organization] - volunteer/grant/partnership

```
[Name],

**[Tool name]** helps nonprofits:

Interested in a brief conversation?

[Your name]
```

## Outreach Sequences

### Sequence 1: Business Prospect (2 weeks)

| Day | Action | Content |
|-----|--------|---------|
| 1 | Initial email | Template 1 |
| 3 | LinkedIn connect | Personal note referencing email |
| 5 | Follow-up email | "Quick follow - still interested?" |
| 7 | Phone call | If contact info available |
| 10 | Final email | "Worth 15 minutes?" |
| 14 | Archive | Mark for future re-engagement |

### Sequence 2: Government (3 weeks)

| Day | Action | Content |
|-----|--------|---------|
| 1 | Initial email | Template 2 |
| 5 | Follow-up | Reference relevant news/policy |
| 10 | LinkedIn/phone | Alternative contact method |
| 14 | Value-add | Send relevant case study/article |
| 21 | Final attempt | "Last follow-up..." |
