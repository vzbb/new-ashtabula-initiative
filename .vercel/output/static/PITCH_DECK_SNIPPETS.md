## Civic Insight Engine

**The Hook:**
> Local government shouldn't require a PhD to understand. Civic Insight Engine uses AI to transform boring meeting minutes into clear, actionable insights — so citizens can stay informed without the homework.

**The Problem:**
Government transparency data is scattered across websites, buried in PDFs, and written in bureaucratic language. Citizens are uninformed and disconnected from local decisions.

**The Solution:**
AI-powered platform that summarizes meetings, tracks community issues, and makes budgets understandable — all in one place.

**The Ask:**
Deploy this for your city and engage citizens like never before.

Showcase your region's potential with data that convinces.

## Permit Whisperer

**The Hook:**
> The permit process shouldn't require a law degree. Permit Whisperer uses AI to translate "I want to build a deck" into "You need these 3 permits, here's what to file, and here's when you'll hear back."

**The Problem:**
Getting permits is confusing. Citizens don't know which permits apply, what documents they need, or how long it takes.

**The Solution:**
AI-guided assistant that tells citizens exactly what permits they need, what to prepare, and tracks their application status.

**The Ask:**
Reduce permit desk calls by 70% with self-service AI guidance.

## Local Grocer Go

Help us scale the local food economy — one zip code at a time.
