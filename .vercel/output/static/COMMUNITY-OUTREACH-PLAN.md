## Outreach Templates

### Template A: Government Stakeholder (Formal)
**Subject:** Civic Technology Partnership — Free Tools for Ashtabula Residents

```
Dear [Name],

I'm reaching out on behalf of Noirsys AI, a local technology initiative working to 
modernize Ashtabula's civic infrastructure through free, accessible digital tools.

We've built prototypes that could help your office:
• permit-whisperer: Interactive guide for residents navigating permits
• zoning-clerk: AI assistant for zoning questions (reduces phone calls)
• [relevant tool]: [specific benefit]

These tools are free to deploy and maintain. We're seeking partners to pilot 
them with real residents and gather feedback.

Would you be open to a 15-minute call to discuss how these might support your work?

Best regards,
Michael A. Vega
Founder, Noirsys AI
contact@noirsys.com
```

### Template B: Business Outreach (Direct)
**Subject:** Free Tool to Connect You with Local Projects

```
Hi [Name],

I'm building a free platform to connect Ashtabula homeowners with verified 
local contractors — and I'd love to include you.

contractor-match features:
• Verified contractor profiles (license, insurance checks)
• Project matching based on your specialties
• Direct quote requests from pre-qualified homeowners
• No fees for contractors during pilot phase

Would you be interested in a 10-minute demo? We're starting with 10 local 
contractors to test the platform.

Thanks,
Michael
```
