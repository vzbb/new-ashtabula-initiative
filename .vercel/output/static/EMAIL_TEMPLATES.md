## Sending Tips

1. **Personalize the [bracketed] fields** — Generic emails get ignored
2. **Send Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM** — Best open rates
3. **Follow up once after 5-7 days** — Then move on
4. **Track opens/clicks** — Use this data to prioritize follow-ups
5. **Keep it under 150 words** — Busy people skim
