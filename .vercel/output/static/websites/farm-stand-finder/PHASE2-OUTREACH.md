## 4. Competitive Intelligence

### LocalHarvest.org
- **Strengths:** 20+ years, extensive database, CSA listings
- **Weaknesses:** Outdated UI, no real-time updates, limited mobile
- **Our angle:** Real-time, mobile-first, Ashtabula-specific

### Farmish App
- **Strengths:** Modern app, social features
- **Weaknesses:** Limited Ashtabula presence, $10/mo for unlimited
- **Our angle:** Free, locally focused, tourist-friendly

### Google Maps
- **Strengths:** Universal, familiar
- **Weaknesses:** Generic, no farm-specific features, outdated hours
- **Our angle:** Farm-specific, real-time updates, discovery focused

**Key Differentiation:**
- **Real-time:** Hours/availability that update instantly
- **Tourist-focused:** Discovery for visitors who don't know local scene
- **U-pick tracking:** Know before you go (field picked out?)
- **Offline mode:** Works in rural areas with poor cell coverage
- **Multi-language:** Spanish support for agriculture workers
- **FREE:** No cost to farmers (vs Farmish $10/mo)

## 5. User Testing Plan

### Target Testers

| Group | Count | Recruitment |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| Farm stand owners | 3-5 | Via market managers |
| Farmers market vendors | 3-5 | At markets |
| Local shoppers | 5-10 | Facebook groups |
| Tourists (Geneva-on-the-Lake) | 3-5 | Visitor areas |
| Families (U-pick users) | 3-5 | At existing U-pick farms |

### Testing Scenarios

1. **Find a farm stand open now** — Can they locate nearby options?
2. **Plan a U-pick trip** — Can they check availability before driving?
3. **Discover new farms** — Can tourists find hidden gems?
4. **Update farm info** — Can farmers update hours easily?
5. **Offline usage** — Does it work without cell service?

## 6. Risk Assessment Matrix

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Farmers don't update info | High | Medium | Simple update process, SMS option, reminders |
| Low initial farm participation | Medium | High | Start with market managers as validators |
| Tourist seasonality | High | Low | Focus on year-round farms + events |
| Competing platform launches | Low | Medium | First-mover advantage, local relationships |
| Data accuracy complaints | Medium | Medium | Clear source attribution, farmer-controlled listings |

## 7. Go/No-Go Decision Tree

```
After outreach:
│
├─► If 3+ markets AND 5+ farms interested:
│   └─► Proceed to Phase 3 (SPEC.md)
│       └─► Full platform with real-time features
│
├─► If 1-2 markets OR 3-4 farms interested:
│   └─► Proceed with limited pilot
│       └─► Focus on core features only
│
├─► If tourism partner (Visit Ashtabula) interested:
│   └─► Proceed with tourist focus
│       └─► Market to visitors as primary use case
│
└─► If minimal interest:
    └─► Pause
        └─► Document learnings, pivot approach
```

### Go Criteria
- [ ] At least 1 farmers market manager commits to promoting
- [ ] OR 5+ farms agree to be listed
- [ ] OR tourism partner agrees to cross-promotion

### No-Go Criteria
- [ ] All market managers decline
- [ ] Farmers cite lack of need (happy with current methods)
- [ ] Tourism partner not interested
