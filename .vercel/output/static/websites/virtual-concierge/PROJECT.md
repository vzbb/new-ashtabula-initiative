# Virtual Concierge

**Status:** 🟡 Phase 1 Complete (Research)  
**Priority:** P2 (Tourism & Community)  
**Target:** Hotels, B&Bs, vacation rentals in Ashtabula County

---

## Problem
Hotels and B&Bs lack modern digital concierge solutions. Guests can't easily find curated local recommendations, leading to missed revenue opportunities and frustrated visitors.

## Solution
White-label virtual concierge platform providing AI-powered local recommendations, booking integration, and revenue sharing for hospitality properties.

## Key Differentiators
- Hyper-local Ashtabula curation (wineries, restaurants, activities)
- Affordable for small B&Bs (free tier available)
- Revenue sharing model (10-20% commissions)
- No app download required

## Market
- 25-35 hotels/motels in county
- 15-25 B&Bs
- 100+ vacation rentals
- ~2M annual visitor days

## Revenue Model
| Tier | Price | Target |
|------|-------|--------|
| Free | $0 | B&Bs, small rentals |
| Pro | $49/mo | Small hotels (10-50 rooms) |
| Business | $149/mo | Larger hotels |
| Enterprise | Custom | Resort chains |

## Tech Stack (Proposed)
- Next.js + Tailwind CSS
- Supabase (database + auth)
- OpenAI GPT-4 + embeddings
- Pinecone vector store
- Vercel hosting
