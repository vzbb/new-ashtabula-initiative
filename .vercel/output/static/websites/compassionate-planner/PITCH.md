# Compassionate Planner — Pitch Materials

## 30-Second Demo Script

**HOOK:**
"Imagine you're a family member trying to honor a loved one's final wishes—but you're overwhelmed by paperwork, emotional decisions, and unclear next steps during one of life's hardest moments."

**DEMO (5 steps, ~30 seconds):**

1. *Type "Johnson Family"* into the name field
2. *Select "Cremation"* as the preference
3. *Click "Generate Summary"*
4. *AI generates in 5 seconds:* a gentle planning confirmation with 2 clear next steps
5. *The summary appears:* comforting tone, actionable guidance, ready to share with family

**WHAT IT DOES:**
AI-powered funeral pre-planning assistant that generates compassionate, personalized planning summaries with clear next steps for grieving families.

**WHO IT'S FOR:**
Funeral homes, memorial services, and hospice care providers serving families during end-of-life planning.

**CTA:**
"Let's schedule a 15-minute call to see how this can help your families navigate planning with dignity and clarity."

---

## Key Features

1. **Gentle AI Summaries** — Compassionate, human-tone messaging automatically generated
2. **Customizable Preferences** — Traditional, Cremation, or Memorial-only options
3. **Instant Output** — 5-second response time for time-sensitive situations
4. **Clear Next Steps** — Each summary includes 2 actionable follow-ups
5. **Professional Branding** — Branded for Ducro Funeral Services, Ashtabula County
6. **Mobile-Responsive** — Accessible on any device families use
7. **Low-Friction Interface** — Minimal inputs for emotionally-charged moments

---

## Value Proposition

**For Families:**
- Reduces decision fatigue during emotional times
- Provides clear, written guidance to share with relatives
- Honors loved ones with respectful, thoughtful communication

**For Funeral Homes:**
- Streamlines pre-planning consultations
- Creates consistent, professional communication
- Differentiates service with modern, AI-assisted support
- Reduces staff time on initial planning discussions
