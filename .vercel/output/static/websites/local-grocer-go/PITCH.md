# Local Grocer Go — Pitch Document

## One-Liner
A hyperlocal food marketplace connecting consumers with nearby farms, farmers markets, and artisan producers — fresh, local, delivered.

---

## 30-Second Demo Script

**Hook:** "You want fresh, local produce, but driving to three different farms and markets every week takes forever..."

**Demo:**
1. **(2s)** Open newashtabula.vercel.app/grocer/
2. **(10s)** "I enter my zip code and see all the local producers nearby — farms, markets, bakeries..."
3. **(10s)** "I can browse seasonal availability, place orders for pickup or delivery, and even subscribe to weekly CSA boxes."
4. **(5s)** "Track my orders, chat with growers, discover new local products."
5. **(3s)** "Farm fresh without the farm runaround."

---

## What It Does
Connects local food producers directly with consumers through discovery, ordering, and fulfillment tools — cutting out intermediaries.

---

## Who It's For
- **Primary:** Health-conscious consumers wanting local food
- **Secondary:** Local farmers and food producers
- **Tertiary:** Restaurants seeking local suppliers

---

## The Problem
Buying local food is hard:
- Don't know what's available nearby
- Multiple stops at different locations
- Inconsistent availability info
- No easy ordering/payment

**Result:** People buy from supermarkets instead of local producers.

---

## The Solution
**Local Grocer Go** makes local easy:
- 🗺️ **Producer Discovery** — Find farms and markets near you
- 📅 **Seasonal Calendars** — Know what's available when
- 🛒 **Online Ordering** — Order for pickup or delivery
- 🔄 **Subscriptions** — Weekly CSA boxes, recurring orders
- 💬 **Direct Messaging** — Chat with your growers

---

## Key Features
1. **Geo-Search** — Find producers within X miles
2. **Product Catalogs** — Browse availability and prices
3. **Order Management** — Cart, checkout, tracking
4. **Producer Dashboard** — Manage inventory and orders
5. **Subscription System** — Recurring CSA boxes

---

## Call to Action
**For Consumers:** "Eat local without the hassle — discover, order, enjoy."

**For Producers:** "Sell direct to customers — keep more of what you earn."

---

## Technical Highlights
- React + Vite frontend
- Node.js backend
- MongoDB database
- Stripe payments
- Real-time inventory
- Mobile app-ready
