## 3. Stakeholder Outreach Templates

### Template A: City Engineering Division Email

**Subject:** Proposal: AI Assistant to Reduce Engineering Inquiry Call Volume

```
Dear City Engineering Team,

My name is [Name], and I'm a local technology consultant working on a pro-bono 
project to help Ashtabula residents better understand engineering and permit 
requirements.

I'm building a free AI-powered "Engineer's Assistant" — a web-based tool that 
answers common homeowner questions about:
- Driveway permits and curb cuts
- Sidewalk repair requirements  
- Drainage and stormwater questions
- Sewer connections and tap-ins
- When permits are needed vs. not needed

The goal is to reduce repetitive phone inquiries to your office while ensuring 
residents get accurate, consistent information 24/7.

Would you be available for a brief 20-30 minute meeting to:
1. Introduce the concept and show a demo
2. Discuss any concerns about accuracy/liability
3. Explore whether the City might endorse or partner on this tool
4. Request access to current fee schedules and permit applications

I'm happy to work around your schedule and come to City Hall at your convenience.

Thank you for your time and for the important work you do for our community.

Best regards,
[Name]
[Phone]
[Email]
```

### Template B: County Building Department Email

**Subject:** Collaborative Opportunity — Digital Permit Guidance Tool for Ashtabula County Residents

```
Dear Ashtabula County Building Department,

I'm developing a free online tool to help residents understand the permit process 
between the City of Ashtabula and Ashtabula County.

The tool would provide clear guidance on:
- When County building permits are required
- The City Zoning → County Building permit workflow
- Documentation requirements
- General timeline expectations

This is intended to complement (not replace) your office's work by helping 
residents come prepared with the right information.

Would you be open to a brief conversation about:
1. How we can ensure accuracy in our guidance
2. Whether you see value in such a tool for residents
3. Possible collaboration or endorsement

I can meet in Jefferson or schedule a phone call at your convenience.

Respectfully,
[Name]
[Contact Info]
```
