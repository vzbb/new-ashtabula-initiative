## 4. Technical Resource Assessment

### Mapping APIs

| Provider | Cost | Pros | Cons | Recommendation |
|----------|------|------|------|----------------|
| **Google Maps Platform** | $7 per 1000 loads (Dynamic Maps) | Best imagery, familiar UI | Expensive at scale | START HERE |
| **Mapbox** | Free tier: 50K loads/mo, then $0.25-1.00 per 1000 | Cheaper, custom styling | Slightly less detail | SCALE OPTION |
| **OpenStreetMap + Leaflet** | Free | Zero cost | Requires self-hosting tiles | BUDGET OPTION |

**Recommendation:** Start with Google Maps for pilot (ease of use), migrate to Mapbox if scale exceeds free tier.

### Fence Drawing Libraries

| Library | Purpose | Complexity |
|---------|---------|------------|
| **react-google-maps** | Google Maps React integration | Low |
| **@react-google-maps/api** | Modern React wrapper | Low |
| **Leaflet.js** | Open-source alternative | Medium |
| **Turf.js** | Geospatial calculations (distance, area) | Medium |

## 5. Market Validation Checklist

### Homeowner Survey (Target: 10+ responses)

**Distribution Channels:**
- [ ] Ashtabula Facebook community groups
- [ ] Nextdoor posts in Ashtabula/Geneva/Dorset
- [ ] Local Reddit (r/Ohio, r/Cleveland)
- [ ] Chamber of Commerce newsletter

**Survey Questions:**

1. Have you gotten a fence quote in the past 2 years? (Y/N)
2. How did you find your fencing contractor? (Friend/Online/Phone book/Drove by)
3. How long did it take to get your first quote? (Same day/1-3 days/1 week+/Never got one)
4. What was most frustrating about the process?
5. Would you use an online tool to get instant pricing before calling contractors? (1-5 scale)
6. What fence type are you most interested in? (Wood/Vinyl/Chain-link/Aluminum)
7. What's your approximate budget range? ($1-2K/$2-5K/$5-10K/$10K+)
8. Would you pay a $25 refundable deposit to schedule a site visit? (Y/N/Maybe)
