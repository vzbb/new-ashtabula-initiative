## 2. Discovery Call Question Sets

### Set A: For Individual Agents (15-20 minutes)

**Opening:**
> "Thank you for taking the time to chat. I'm researching how local real estate professionals market properties and I came across your impressive sales record. I'd love to understand your current approach and any challenges you're facing with property marketing."

**Current State Questions:**
1. How do you currently handle photography and property marketing for your listings?
2. Have you ever used virtual tours or 3D walkthroughs? What was your experience?
3. What percentage of your listings would you say are to out-of-area buyers?
4. How do you typically compete for listings against other agents in the area?

**Pain Point Questions:**
5. What's the biggest challenge you face when marketing a property?
6. How do you feel about the cost of professional photography and virtual tour services?
7. Have you ever lost a listing to an agent who offered better marketing technology?
8. What's your current monthly budget for marketing tools and services?

**Desired State Questions:**
9. If you could wave a wand and improve one thing about how you showcase properties, what would it be?
10. What would make you consider switching to a new virtual tour solution?
11. How important is having your own branded marketing vs using third-party platforms like Zillow?

**Closing Questions:**
12. Would you be interested in participating in a free pilot program for a new local virtual tour platform?
13. Who else in your network might benefit from this type of tool?

---

### Set B: For Brokerage Owners/Managers (20-30 minutes)

**Opening:**
> "I appreciate you making time for this conversation. I'm exploring how local brokerages are leveraging technology to support their agents and win listings. Your brokerage has an excellent reputation in the county, and I'd value your insights."

**Business Overview:**
1. How many agents do you currently have, and what's your typical annual transaction volume?
2. What technology tools do you currently provide or recommend to your agents?
3. Do you have a standardized marketing package that agents must use or recommend?

**Technology Adoption:**
4. What percentage of your agents currently use virtual tours for their listings?
5. Have you evaluated solutions like Matterport or Zillow 3D Home for your agents?
6. What barriers have prevented wider adoption of virtual tour technology?

**Competitive Positioning:**
7. How does your brokerage differentiate from competitors when recruiting agents?
8. What role does technology play in your agent recruitment and retention?
9. How do you compete against larger national brands or discount brokerages?

**Decision Making:**
10. What factors would influence your decision to adopt a new marketing platform for your agents?
11. Would you prefer a brokerage-level subscription or individual agent subscriptions?
12. What level of training and support would you expect for a new platform?

**Partnership Opportunity:**
13. Would you be open to a partnership where we offer your agents a preferred rate in exchange for feedback and testimonials?
14. Could we schedule a demo for you and a few of your top agents?

---

### Set C: For Property Managers/Developers (20-30 minutes)

**Opening:**
> "Thanks for connecting with me. I understand you manage/ develop properties in the area, and I'm researching better ways to market rental units and new developments. I'd love to learn about your current marketing process."

**Current Marketing:**
1. How do you currently market vacant units or pre-construction properties?
2. What channels drive the most qualified leads for you? (Zillow, Facebook, MLS, referrals)
3. How do you handle showings for occupied properties?

**Challenges:**
4. What percentage of your inquiries come from out-of-area prospects?
5. How much time do you or your staff spend on property showings?
6. What's your current cost per lead or cost per lease signed?

**Technology Interest:**
7. Have you considered virtual tours to pre-qualify prospects before in-person showings?
8. What would a virtual tour solution need to include for you to consider it?
9. Would you be interested in virtual staging for unfurnished units?

**Decision Factors:**
10. What's your typical marketing budget per unit or per development?
11. Who makes technology decisions for your organization?
12. What timeline are you working with for implementing new marketing tools?

## 4. Partnership Approach: Free Pilot Program

### Pilot Program Structure

**Name:** "Founding Agent" Program

**What We Offer (Free for 90 Days):**
1. Full access to Professional tier features ($79/month value)
2. Up to 10 active property listings
3. Unlimited 360° virtual tours
4. Branded photo galleries
5. Priority email and phone support
6. Complimentary onboarding and training session
7. Dedicated account manager

**What We Ask in Return:**
1. One 30-minute feedback call per month
2. Permission to use anonymized usage data for product improvement
3. Testimonial (if satisfied) at end of pilot
4. Introduction to 2-3 other agents who might benefit

**Pilot Program Limits:**
- Maximum 10 agents for first cohort
- Ashtabula County agents only (first phase)
- Must commit to listing at least 2 properties during pilot

## 7. Key Messaging Guidelines

### Do's
- ✅ Emphasize LOCAL presence and support
- ✅ Focus on affordability vs. Matterport
- ✅ Highlight agent branding (not third-party platforms)
- ✅ Offer tangible value (free pilot, complimentary tour)
- ✅ Reference Ashtabula County market specifically
- ✅ Keep emails concise and scannable

### Don'ts
- ❌ Don't oversell or use hype language
- ❌ Don't criticize competitors directly
- ❌ Don't ask for too much time upfront
- ❌ Don't use generic templates without personalization
- ❌ Don't follow up more than twice without response
