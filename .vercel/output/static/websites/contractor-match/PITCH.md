# Contractor Match — Pitch Document

## One-Liner
Smart contractor matching platform that connects homeowners with verified local pros — get quotes, compare reviews, and hire with confidence.

---

## 30-Second Demo Script

**Hook:** "Your furnace just died in February, and you need a reliable HVAC tech ASAP — but who can you trust?"

**Demo:**
1. **(2s)** Open newashtabula.vercel.app/contractors/
2. **(10s)** "I enter what I need — 'furnace repair' — and my zip code..."
3. **(10s)** "I see verified contractors in my area, their ratings, specialties, and I can request quotes from multiple pros at once."
4. **(5s)** "Compare quotes, read reviews, check licenses — all in one place."
5. **(3s)** "Find the right pro, right now."

---

## What It Does
Matches homeowners with verified local contractors through smart filtering, quote comparison, and reputation verification.

---

## Who It's For
- **Primary:** Homeowners needing home services
- **Secondary:** Property managers, landlords
- **Tertiary:** Contractors seeking new customers

---

## The Problem
Finding contractors is stressful:
- Don't know who's reliable
- Hard to compare prices
- Unclear credentials
- Scheduling nightmares

**Result:** Homeowners delay repairs or hire the wrong person.

---

## The Solution
**Contractor Match** makes hiring easy:
- 🔍 **Smart Matching** — Describe your project, get matched pros
- ⭐ **Verified Reviews** — Real feedback from real customers
- 📋 **License Verification** — Check credentials upfront
- 💰 **Quote Comparison** — Multiple bids, one platform
- 📅 **Scheduling** — Book directly through the app

---

## Key Features
1. **Project-Based Matching** — AI understands what you need
2. **Pro Profiles** — Portfolio, reviews, credentials
3. **Instant Quotes** — Request bids from multiple contractors
4. **Secure Messaging** — Chat with pros before hiring
5. **Job Tracking** — Monitor progress, approve milestones

---

## Call to Action
**For Homeowners:** "Stop guessing — hire verified pros with confidence."

**For Contractors:** "Get qualified leads without the marketing headache."
