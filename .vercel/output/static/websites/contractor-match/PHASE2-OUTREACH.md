## 4. Verification Process Design

### Tier 1: Licensed Professional

**Requirements:**
- [ ] Active Ohio OCILB license (document upload)
- [ ] Background check (GoodHire/Checkr)
- [ ] General liability insurance ($500K+)
- [ ] Workers comp (if applicable)
- [ ] 2+ local references
- [ ] Profile completion (photos, description)

**Badge:** "State Licensed & Verified"

### Tier 2: Verified Handyman

**Requirements:**
- [ ] Background check
- [ ] General liability insurance
- [ ] 3+ local references
- [ ] No license required (handyman exemption)

**Badge:** "Background Checked & Insured"

### Verification Workflow

```
Contractor applies
    ↓
Document upload (license, insurance)
    ↓
Background check initiated (platform pays)
    ↓
Manual review (24-48 hours)
    ↓
Approval / Rejection with feedback
    ↓
Profile published
    ↓
Ongoing: Annual re-verification
```

## 5. Competitive Intelligence

### Angi (formerly Angie's List)
- **Pricing:** $300+/month for contractors
- **Vetting:** Reviews only, weak license verification
- **Weakness:** Expensive, low local accountability
- **Our advantage:** Free tier, municipal partnership, better verification

### HomeAdvisor
- **Pricing:** $15-100 per lead
- **Vetting:** Optional background check
- **Weakness:** Lead quality complaints, contractor spam
- **Our advantage:** Pre-qualified leads, no spam, local focus

### Facebook Marketplace
- **Pricing:** Free
- **Vetting:** None
- **Weakness:** No trust mechanism, scams common
- **Our advantage:** Verified contractors, dispute resolution, insurance

**Key Differentiation:**
- **Municipal partnership** — City endorsement no competitor has
- **Two-tier verification** — Licensed + handyman options
- **Payment protection** — Escrow reduces non-payment risk
- **Local only** — No out-of-town competition
- **Free tier** — 6 months no cost vs. $300+/mo Angi
