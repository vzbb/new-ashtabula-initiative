## Phase 1: Research & Recon ✅ COMPLETE

### Deliverable: PHASE1-RESEARCH.md
- **File:** `PHASE1-RESEARCH.md` (14.6KB)
- **Date:** 2026-02-18
- **Key Findings:**
  - **Problem:** Ashtabula homeowners rely on phone-only HVAC booking; no online scheduling exists locally
  - **Market Gap:** 10+ local HVAC contractors, ZERO offer online booking
  - **Personas:** 4 types — Preventive Patty, Emergency Eddie, Landlord Larry, New Neighbor Nancy
  - **Competitors:** ServiceTitan, Jobber, Housecall Pro (all contractor-side only); Angi/HomeAdvisor (expensive lead gen)
  - **Pricing:** Maintenance plans $150-300/year; single tuneup $65-200; emergency repairs 2-5x premium
  - **Contractors Mapped:** HAVE, Blank Heating, Hearn, Ryan, Ogram + 5+ independents
  - **Seasonality:** Peak demand Dec-Feb (heating) and Jul-Aug (cooling) — Lake Erie climate severity
  - **Differentiation:** Ashtabula-only focus, online booking for homeowners, maintenance reminders, transparent pricing
  - **Revenue Model:** Free homeowners, $49-99/mo contractor tiers, $25/mo featured listings
  - **Open Questions:** 10 blockers for Phase 2 including contractor willingness, market size, homeowner behavior
