# HVAC Tune-Up

**Status:** 🟡 Phase 1 Complete (Research)  
**Priority:** P2 (Home Services)  
**Target:** HVAC contractors and homeowners in Ashtabula County

---

## Problem
HVAC companies lose 30-40% of maintenance contract renewals. Homeowners forget seasonal tune-ups. Phone tag wastes time. Emergency repairs cost 3-5x more than preventive maintenance.

## Solution
"OpenTable for HVAC maintenance" — book seasonal tune-ups online, automatic reminders, price comparison.

## Market
- 25-40 HVAC contractors in Ashtabula County
- 60,000-80,000 annual tune-ups needed
- $4.8M - $12M market value
- Average tune-up: $80-$150

## Revenue Model
- Booking fee: $5-10 per tune-up
- Lead commission: 5-10%
- Featured contractor: $50-100/mo
