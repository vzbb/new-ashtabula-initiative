# HVAC Tune-Up — Phase 1 Research
## Seasonal HVAC Maintenance Booking for Ashtabula County

**Date:** February 19, 2026  
**Status:** Phase 1 Research  
**Next:** Phase 2 Resource Procurement

---

## 1. Problem Statement

**Pain Points:**
- HVAC companies lose 30-40% of maintenance contract renewals due to poor follow-up
- Homeowners forget to schedule seasonal tune-ups (spring AC, fall furnace)
- Phone tag wastes office staff time
- No easy way to compare HVAC service prices
- Emergency repairs cost 3-5x more than preventive maintenance

---

## 2. Market Analysis

**Ashtabula County HVAC Market:**
- **Housing units:** ~45,000
- **HVAC contractors:** 25-40 (independent + franchises)
- **Annual tune-ups needed:** 60,000-80,000 (2x per home)
- **Average tune-up price:** $80-$150
- **Total addressable market:** $4.8M - $12M annually

**Key Contractors:**
- One Hour Heating & Air Conditioning
- Local independents (15-25)
- Home warranty companies

---

## 3. Competitor Analysis

| Platform | Model | Cost | Weakness |
|----------|-------|------|----------|
| **HomeAdvisor** | Lead gen | $50-100/lead | Expensive, shared leads |
| **Thumbtack** | Marketplace | Commission | Price competition |
| **Company websites** | Direct | Free | No comparison |
| **Angi** | Membership | Subscription | Generic |

**Gap:** No Ashtabula-specific HVAC booking with seasonal reminders and price transparency.

---

## 4. User Personas

**Homeowner Hannah:** Needs seasonal tune-up reminders, wants transparent pricing

**HVAC Owner Harry:** Wants steady maintenance contracts, less phone tag

---

## 5. Solution Concept

**"OpenTable for HVAC maintenance" — book seasonal tune-ups online, automatic reminders, price comparison.**

**Features:**
- Seasonal booking (spring AC, fall furnace)
- Price comparison by contractor
- Maintenance contract management
- Automatic reminder system
- Emergency service flag

---

## 6. Tech Stack

- Next.js + Supabase
- Twilio (SMS reminders)
- Stripe (payments)

---
