## Phase 1 Summary

**Market validated:**
- 400-650 vacation rental units in Ashtabula County
- $8M-$15M annual rental revenue
- Gap: No affordable booking solution for small operators (1-5 properties)

**Competitors analyzed:**
- Airbnb (17% total fees)
- VRBO ($499/year or 8%)
- OwnerRez/Lodgify ($29-$250/month)
- **Gap:** No local, Stripe-integrated solution under $50/month

**4 Personas defined:**
1. Cottage Owner Cathy (retired, 2 properties)
2. Harbor Host Henry (Airbnb refugee, wants control)
3. Property Manager Pam (8-12 properties)
4. Vacation Renter Rachel (young family)

**Business model:**
- Basic: $19/month (1 property)
- Pro: $39/month (3 properties)
- Manager: $79/month (10 properties)
- Projected MRR at 50 properties: $3,450
