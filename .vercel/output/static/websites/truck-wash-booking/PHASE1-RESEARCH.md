## 1. Problem Statement

### The Pain Point
Fleet operators and truck owners in Ashtabula County struggle with vehicle washing:
- **Inconsistent availability:** Truck washes have limited hours, long waits
- **No booking system:** First-come-first-served wastes driver time
- **Quality unknown:** No reviews or standards for wash quality
- **Fleet coordination:** Hard to schedule multiple trucks efficiently
- **Location gaps:** Few options near industrial areas and highways

### Impact on Fleet Operators
- **Driver downtime:** 1-2 hours waiting at wash facility
- **Scheduling chaos:** Can't plan wash around delivery routes
- **Compliance risk:** Dirty trucks fail DOT inspections
- **Inefficient routing:** Drive out of way to find open wash

### Impact on Independent Truckers
- **Lost revenue:** Time spent washing = time not hauling
- **Uncertain costs:** Prices vary, no transparency
- **Weekend problems:** Most washes closed Sat-Sun or limited hours
- **No mobile option:** Must drive to facility (fuel + time cost)

## 4. User Personas

### Primary: "Fleet Frank" (Fleet Manager)
- **Demographics:** 40-60, manages 10-50 truck fleet
- **Business:** Local delivery, construction, or logistics company
- **Tech comfort:** Moderate (uses fleet management software)
- **Pain points:**
  - Coordinating washes for 20+ trucks is chaotic
  - Can't schedule washes around delivery routes
  - No visibility into which trucks are clean
  - Drivers waste time waiting at wash facilities
- **Needs:** Bulk booking, route-optimized scheduling, fleet wash status

### Primary: "Independent Ivan" (Owner-Operator)
- **Demographics:** 30-55, owns 1-2 trucks
- **Business:** Independent hauling, often multi-state
- **Tech comfort:** Moderate-High (uses apps for loads, fuel)
- **Pain points:**
  - Hard to find wash when traveling through unfamiliar areas
  - Blue Beacon often has 30+ min wait
  - Weekend washes especially difficult
  - Price uncertainty (surprise upcharges)
- **Needs:** Book ahead, see wait times, know price upfront

### Secondary: "Dispatcher Diana" (Logistics Coordinator)
- **Demographics:** 30-50, dispatch office
- **Role:** Routes drivers, manages schedules
- **Tech comfort:** High
- **Pain points:**
  - Drivers calling asking where to wash
  - Can't plan wash stops into routes
  - Last-minute wash needs disrupt schedule
- **Needs:** Route planning with wash stops, availability API

### Secondary: "Weekend Warren" (Weekend Warrior)
- **Demographics:** 35-60, RV owner or classic truck enthusiast
- **Needs:** Quality wash/detail, appointment-based
- **Pain points:**
  - RVs too big for standard washes
  - Weekend availability limited
  - Wants quality, not just speed

## 5. Solution Concept

### Core Concept
**"OpenTable for truck washing — book ahead, skip the line, know your price."**

### Key Features

**For Truck Drivers/Fleet Managers:**
1. **Real-Time Availability** — See which washes have open slots
2. **Book Ahead** — Reserve time slot, skip the wait
3. **Price Transparency** — Know cost before arrival
4. **Route Integration** — Find washes on your route
5. **Fleet Dashboard** — Manage multiple trucks, schedules, receipts
6. **Quality Ratings** — See reviews from other truckers
7. **Mobile Payments** — Pay in app, no cash handling

**For Wash Facilities:**
1. **Booking Management** — Digital calendar, reduce no-shows
2. **Fleet Contracts** — Recurring bookings, guaranteed revenue
3. **Dynamic Pricing** — Adjust prices based on demand
4. **Customer Insights** — See who's coming, truck types
5. **Marketing** — Discoverable by new customers
6. **Reviews Management** — Respond to feedback

**For Mobile Wash Operators:**
1. **On-Demand Requests** — Accept jobs near current location
2. **Route Optimization** — Group nearby jobs efficiently
3. **Service Area Definition** — Set geographic boundaries
4. **Mobile Payments** — Integrated billing

### Differentiation

| Feature | Truck Wash Booking | Blue Beacon | Mobile Detailers |
|---------|-------------------|-------------|------------------|
| Advance booking | ✓ | ✗ | Phone only |
| Price transparency | ✓ | Partial | Varies |
| Fleet management | ✓ | ✗ | ✗ |
| Quality ratings | ✓ | ✗ | Limited |
| Route planning | ✓ | ✗ | ✗ |
| Mobile on-demand | ✓ (future) | ✗ | Limited |
| Multiple locations | ✓ | Single chain | Single operator |

## 6. Tech Stack Options

### Recommended: Next.js + Supabase + Stripe
- **Frontend:** Next.js 14, Tailwind CSS
- **Backend:** Supabase (PostgreSQL)
- **Payments:** Stripe Connect (split payments to wash operators)
- **Mapping:** Google Maps API
- **Notifications:** Twilio (SMS), SendGrid (email)
- **Hosting:** Vercel
- **Cost:** ~$50-100/mo (scales with transactions)

### Alternative: Marketplace Model (Two-Sided)
- **Platform:** React Native app (drivers) + Web dashboard (wash operators)
- **Backend:** Firebase
- **Payments:** Stripe Connect
- **Cost:** ~$80-150/mo

## 7. Revenue Model

### Transaction-Based (Recommended)

| Revenue Stream | Rate | Notes |
|----------------|------|-------|
| **Booking fee** | $2-5 per wash | Paid by driver |
| **Commission** | 5-10% of wash cost | Paid by wash facility |
| **Fleet subscription** | $49/mo | Unlimited bookings, fleet features |
| **Priority placement** | $25/mo | Featured listing for wash facilities |

### Example Economics (Single Wash):**
- Wash cost: $75
- Commission (8%): $6
- Booking fee: $3
- **Platform revenue: $9 per wash**

### Scale Targets:**
- 100 washes/day = $900/day = $27,000/mo
- 500 washes/day = $4,500/day = $135,000/mo

## 9. Risk Assessment

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Wash facilities won't join | Medium | High | Start with 1-2 willing partners, prove value |
| Drivers don't book ahead | Medium | Medium | Incentivize (discounts), educate on time savings |

| Payment disputes | Medium | Medium | Clear service standards, photo proof |
| Seasonal demand (winter salt) | High | Low | Higher prices in peak, subscription model |
