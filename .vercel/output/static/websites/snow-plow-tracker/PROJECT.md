# Snow Plow Tracker

**Status:** 🟡 Phase 1 Complete (Research)  
**Priority:** P2 (Business Tools — Seasonal)  
**Target:** Snow plow operators in Ashtabula County

---

## Problem
Plow operators waste 15-30% of their day on phone calls. Homeowners don't know when their driveway will be cleared. No visibility into route status causes frustration for everyone.

## Solution
Real-time snow plow tracking with route optimization, customer notifications, and proof of service. "Uber for snow plowing."

## Key Differentiators
- Live GPS tracking visible to customers
- Route optimization for multiple trucks
- Photo proof of service (billing disputes)
- Municipal public maps (road clearing status)
- Affordable for solo operators ($29/mo)

## Market
- 80-120 plow operators in Ashtabula County
- ~10,000 residential snow removal contracts
- $6M - $12M annual addressable market
- 70-100+ inches of Lake Effect snow/year

## Revenue Model
| Tier | Price | Target |
|------|-------|--------|
| Free | $0 | Testing (1 truck, 20 customers) |
| Pro | $29/mo | Solo operators |
| Business | $79/mo | Small companies (5 trucks) |
| Enterprise | $199/mo | Large companies |
| Municipal | Custom | Townships/Cities |

## Tech Stack (Proposed)
- React Native (mobile app)
- Firebase (backend + auth)
- Mapbox (mapping)
- Twilio (SMS)
