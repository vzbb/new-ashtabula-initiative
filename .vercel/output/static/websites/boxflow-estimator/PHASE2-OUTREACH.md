## 3. Discovery Questions

### For Wineries

**Opening Context:**
"I want to understand how you handle shipping today so we can build 
something that actually helps. There are no wrong answers — I'm just 
gathering honest perspectives."

**Current State:**
1. How many shipping orders do you process per week/month?
2. Walk me through your packing process — who does it, how do they decide 
   which box to use?
3. What box sizes do you keep in inventory?
4. How do you handle mixed-case orders (e.g., 3 reds, 2 whites, 1 rosé)?

**Pain Points:**
5. What percentage of your shipments arrive damaged? How do you handle 
   those claims?
6. Have you noticed shipping costs increasing? What's your monthly spend 
   approximately?
7. What's the most frustrating part of your shipping process?
8. Tell me about a recent shipping "disaster" — what happened?

**Current Tools:**
9. What software do you use for shipping? (ShipStation, Shopify, manual?)
10. How do you currently calculate shipping costs for customers at checkout?
11. Have you tried any packaging optimization tools before?

**Decision Making:**
12. Who decides on shipping process changes? (Owner, operations manager?)
13. What would it take for you to try a new tool?
14. What would make you stop using a tool after trying it?

**Desired Outcomes:**
15. If you could wave a wand and fix one thing about shipping, what would 
    it be?
16. What metrics do you track for shipping? (Cost per package, damage rate?)
17. What would "successful" shipping look like for you?

---

### For Manufacturers/Distributors

**Current State:**
1. What types of products do you ship most often?
2. What percentage of your shipments are B2B vs. direct-to-consumer?
3. How many different box/SKU configurations do you manage?
4. Do you use custom packaging or stock boxes?

**Pain Points:**
5. What's your biggest shipping cost driver? (Freight, materials, labor?)
6. How do you handle odd-shaped or oversized items?
7. What percentage of your shipments are LTL (Less Than Truckload)?
8. Have you done any packaging optimization analysis before?

**Process:**
9. Who on your team is responsible for packing decisions?
10. How long does it take to train a new warehouse employee on packing 
    procedures?
11. Do you quote shipping costs to customers before they order? How?

**Technology:**
12. What systems do you currently use? (ERP, WMS, shipping software?)
13. How do you feel about cloud-based software vs. on-premise?
14. What integrations would be essential? (QuickBooks, NetSuite, etc.)
