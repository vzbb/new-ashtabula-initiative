## 3. User Interview Plan

### 3.1 Business Owner Interviews (Target: 10-15)

**Objective:** Validate pain points, pricing tolerance, feature priorities

**Interview Script:**

```
INTRODUCTION (2 minutes)
"Thanks for taking the time. I'm researching how local service businesses 
handle appointments. This isn't a sales call — we're building a free tool 
and want to make sure it actually solves real problems."

BACKGROUND (3 minutes)
1. Tell me about your business — what services do you offer?
2. How long have you been in operation?
3. How many appointments do you handle per week?

CURRENT PROCESS (5 minutes)
4. Walk me through how a customer books an appointment today.
   - Probe: What works well?
   - Probe: What's frustrating?
5. How do you handle reminders?
   - Probe: What percentage of customers no-show?
6. Have you tried online scheduling before? What happened?

PAIN POINTS (5 minutes)
7. What takes up most of your time related to scheduling?
8. If you could wave a wand and fix one scheduling problem, what would it be?
9. How much time do you spend on the phone booking appointments?

SOLUTION REACTION (5 minutes)
10. If there were a free tool that let customers book online 24/7, how 
    interested would you be?
    - Scale: 1-10
11. What features would be most important to you?
    [Show feature list: online booking, reminders, calendar sync, mobile app]
12. What would make you hesitant to try it?

PRICING (3 minutes)
13. If the basic tool were free, would you use it?
14. What would you pay for SMS reminders?
15. What would you pay for multi-staff scheduling?

CLOSE (2 minutes)
"Thanks so much for your time. We're planning to launch in [month]. 
Would you be interested in being a beta tester?"
```

**Compensation:** $25 gift card for 20-minute interview

### 3.2 Customer Survey (Target: 50+ responses)

**Survey Title:** "How do you book local services?"

**Questions:**
1. How do you prefer to book appointments? (Phone / Online / Text / Walk-in)
2. Have you abandoned a booking because you couldn't book online? (Y/N)
3. How important are appointment reminders to you? (1-5 scale)
4. What types of local services do you use? (Check all: Hair, Auto, Home repair, etc.)
5. Would you use a website that lets you book local appointments online? (Y/N/Maybe)
6. Age range? (18-24 / 25-34 / 35-44 / 45-54 / 55+)
7. Zip code?

**Distribution:** Facebook groups, Chamber newsletter, local Reddit
