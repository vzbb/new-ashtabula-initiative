# Insta-Book — Phase 1 Research
## Instant Booking Platform for Local Services

**Date:** February 19, 2026  
**Status:** Phase 1 Research  
**Next:** Phase 2 Resource Procurement

---

## 1. Problem Statement

**Pain Points:**
- Small businesses lose customers due to phone tag
- No-shows waste appointment slots
- Customers want instant booking, not call-backs
- No unified booking platform for Ashtabula services
- Businesses can't afford expensive scheduling software

---

## 2. Market Analysis

**Service Businesses in Ashtabula:**
- **Salons/barbers:** 50-75
- **Massage/therapy:** 15-25
- **Consultants:** 30-50
- **Home services:** 100-200
- **Pet services:** 20-30
- **Tutors:** 20-40

**Booking Volume:**
- Average appointments/day per business: 5-15
- Total daily appointments: 1,500-3,000
- No-show rate: 15-25%

---

## 3. Competitor Analysis

| Platform | Price | Limitation |
|----------|-------|------------|
| **Calendly** | Free-$12/mo | Generic, no local focus |
| **Square Appointments** | Free-$90/mo | Payment required |
| **Acuity** | $16-$61/mo | Expensive for small biz |
| **Booksy** | $29.99/mo | Beauty only |

**Gap:** No local, affordable, cross-category booking platform.

---

## 4. Solution

**"Calendly for Ashtabula" — simple booking widget any local business can embed.**

**Features:**
- Free tier (basic booking)
- SMS reminders (reduce no-shows)
- Payment integration (optional)
- Service categories
- Customer reviews
