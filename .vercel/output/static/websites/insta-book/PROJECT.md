## Problem
Phone tag loses customers. No-shows waste slots. Small businesses can't afford expensive scheduling software.

## Solution
"Calendly for Ashtabula" — simple booking widget for local businesses.

## Market
- 250-400 service businesses
- 1,500-3,000 daily appointments
- 15-25% no-show rate

## Revenue

- Pro: $19/mo unlimited
- Business: $49/mo team
