## Key Features

1. **AI Trip Planner** — Generates custom itineraries based on date, party size, and season using Gemini API
2. **Captain Profiles** — Featured listings with specialties (Walleye, Perch, Steelhead) and trust indicators
3. **Real-Time Availability** — Date-based planning with instant recommendations
4. **Safety Integration** — Automatic safety notes and USCG compliance badges
5. **Mobile Responsive** — Optimized for booking on-the-go at the marina or dock
6. **PDF Export** — Downloadable trip plans for sharing with your group
7. **Local Expertise** — Highlights Ashtabula Harbor as the "Walleye Capital of the World"

## Value Proposition

### For Charter Captains
- **Increased Visibility** — Get discovered by visiting anglers who don't know local operators
- **Streamlined Booking** — AI handles initial trip planning, reducing back-and-forth calls
- **Trust Building** — USCG badges and ratings establish credibility immediately
- **Peak Season Ready** — Capture bookings when anglers are actively planning

### For Anglers
- **Zero Research Hassle** — AI creates the perfect itinerary instantly
- **Confidence in Booking** — Verified captains with clear specialties and ratings
- **Trip Optimization** — Season-aware recommendations for best fishing windows

### For Tourism/Economic Development
- **Destination Marketing** — Positions Ashtabula as a premier fishing destination
