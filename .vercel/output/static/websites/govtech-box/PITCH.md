# GovTech-in-a-Box — Pitch Document

## 30-Second Demo Script

"Imagine you're the clerk for Lenox Township. You've got 400 residents who need to pay permits, request records, and get updates—but your website hasn't been updated since 2012, half the forms are PDFs that don't work on phones, and you're fielding the same phone calls every day.

Watch this. I enter Lenox Township and click Generate Plan. In 5 seconds, Gemini gives me a 3-step rollout plan with timeline estimates: digitize your top 5 forms, launch a resident portal, and automate status notifications. No consultants, no $50,000 RFP, no six-month timeline.

Instead of feeling overwhelmed by modernization, you have a clear, actionable path to better resident service. That's GovTech-in-a-Box—modern township services in weeks, not years."

---

## What/Who/CTA

**What it is:** An AI-powered planning tool that generates customized GovTech rollout plans for small municipalities.

**Who it's for:** Township clerks, village administrators, county IT directors, and local government officials in Ashtabula County.

**Call to Action:** "Let's run this for your township and see what your first 30 days of modernization could look like—no commitment required."

---

## Key Features

1. **Instant Rollout Plans** — Customized 3-step implementation with timelines
2. **Resident-First Design** — Plans prioritize services residents use most
3. **Budget-Conscious** — Focuses on high-impact, low-cost solutions
4. **Standardized Workflows** — Reduces administrative burden through automation
5. **Trust-Building** — Clean, modern interfaces increase resident confidence
6. **Gemini AI Powered** — Plans tailored to your township's specific needs

---

## Value Proposition

For small municipalities that know they need to modernize but don't know where to start, GovTech-in-a-Box transforms digital transformation from an intimidating, expensive undertaking into a clear, step-by-step plan—delivering better resident service in weeks.

## Demo Steps

1. **Set the Scene** — "Lenox Township, 400 residents, website from 2012..."
2. **Enter Township** — Input township name
3. **Generate** — Click "Generate Plan" button
4. **Review Output** — Show the 3-step plan with timeline
5. **Close** — "This is your roadmap to modern services—let's talk budget"

## Technical Notes

- **API:** Google Gemini 1.5 Flash
- **Setup:** VITE_GEMINI_API_KEY environment variable required
- **No data storage** — Township info processed via API, not retained
