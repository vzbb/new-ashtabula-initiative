## 4. Competitive Intelligence

### EvoGov / Revize
- **Pricing:** Custom quotes, typically $500+/month
- **Target:** Mid-to-large cities
- **Weakness:** Too complex/expensive for townships
- **Our angle:** Affordable, simple, township-focused

### Google Workspace Government
- **Pricing:** 71% off standard via GSA
- **Strengths:** Familiar interface, robust
- **Weakness:** Self-serve, no gov-specific setup
- **Our angle:** Turn-key, compliant, supported

### Microsoft 365 GCC
- **Pricing:** $20-35/user/month
- **Strengths:** Enterprise-grade
- **Weakness:** Complex licensing, overkill for townships
- **Our angle:** Simpler, cheaper, designed for small gov

**Key Differentiation:**
- **Affordable:** 1/3 the cost of enterprise solutions
- **Designed for townships:** Not repurposed enterprise tools
- **Compliance built-in:** Ohio Sunshine Law, records retention
- **Local support:** Ashtabula County-based assistance
- **Turn-key:** We handle setup, migration, training

### Common Objections & Responses

**"We can't afford it"**
→ "At $99/month, it costs less than most townships spend on postage. We can also explore county partnership for volume pricing."

**"We've always done it this way"**
→ "Personal email creates liability. With Sunshine Laws, official emails are public records — personal accounts don't provide the archiving you need."

**"We don't have IT staff"**
→ "That's exactly why we built GovTech Box. We handle all technical work. You just use email and update your website like using Facebook."

**"What if it doesn't work?"**
→ "No long-term contract. Cancel anytime. Plus we offer 60-day money-back guarantee."
