## 3. Discovery Call Questions by Stakeholder Type

### Historical Society / Museum Questions

**Mission & Content (10 min)**
1. What do you consider the most important story visitors should know about [site]?
2. Are there common misconceptions about this site's history that we should correct?
3. What primary sources do you have that could inform our narration? (letters, photos, diaries)
4. Are there stories that don't fit on plaques but would work well in audio format?
5. How do you currently handle school groups? Could this extend that reach?

**Partnership Structure (10 min)**
6. Would you be willing to review tour scripts for historical accuracy?
7. What attribution requirements do you have for archival photos/content?
8. Would you prefer a formal MOU or informal collaboration?
9. Are there funding opportunities (grants) we should pursue together?
10. Who else should we talk to about Ashtabula history?

**Logistics (5 min)**
11. What are your busiest visitor times? (so we don't conflict)
12. Are there areas of your property where photography/audio would be restricted?
13. Do you have existing tour scripts we could adapt (with credit)?

### Tourism Board / CVB Questions

**Visitor Data (10 min)**
1. What are the top 3 reasons people visit Ashtabula County?
2. How long is the average visitor stay? (overnight vs. day trip)
3. What visitor demographics are you trying to grow?
4. Are there underserved visitor segments we should target?
5. What questions do visitors ask most at your visitor center?

**Partnership & Distribution (10 min)**
6. Would you distribute Pocket Historian materials at your visitor center?
7. Are there co-marketing opportunities with existing campaigns?
8. What metrics would you want to track from this partnership?
9. Are there other tourism tech initiatives we should align with?
10. Could this be featured in your visitor guide or website?

**Content Priorities (5 min)**
11. If you could have one walking tour created first, what would it be?
12. Are there sites you wish visitors would spend more time at?
13. What seasonal considerations should we build for?

### Business / Downtown Partner Questions

**Foot Traffic & Experience (10 min)**
1. When do you see the most foot traffic on Bridge Street/Main Avenue?
2. What do visitors seem most curious about when they stop in?
3. Are there slow periods where a walking tour might help drive traffic?
4. What historical stories do you share with curious customers?

**Collaboration (10 min)**
5. Would you display a QR code for the walking tour?
6. Are there cross-promotion opportunities with other businesses?
7. Would you be interested in a "tour + discount" model in the future?
8. What concerns do you have about a digital tour in your area?

**Logistics (5 min)**
9. Are there accessibility concerns we should address in the tour route?
10. Who else on Bridge Street/Main Avenue should we talk to?
