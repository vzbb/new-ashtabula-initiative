# Eligibility Pro — Pitch Materials

## 30-Second Demo Script

**HOOK:**
"Imagine you're a benefits counselor trying to match a struggling family with the right assistance programs—but every case is different, and you're juggling paperwork, phone calls, and complex eligibility rules."

**DEMO (5 steps, ~30 seconds):**

1. *Enter "3"* for household size, *"2200"* for income, *"Yes"* for utilities
2. *Type "Heat assistance"* as primary need
3. *Click "Run Screener"*
4. *AI processes 4 data points:* cross-references programs, income limits, utility assistance
5. *Detailed result appears:* Yes/Maybe/No eligibility + 3 specific programs + next steps

**WHAT IT DOES:**
Advanced AI eligibility screener that analyzes household details, utility responsibilities, and specific needs to route clients to the optimal benefit programs faster.

**WHO IT'S FOR:**
Community action agencies, benefits counselors, and social services organizations handling complex case intake and program matching.

**CTA:**
"Let's run a live case from your current caseload—I'll show you how it reduces intake time by 60% and improves program match accuracy."

---

## Key Features

1. **4-Factor Screening** — Household, income, utilities, and specific needs
2. **Program Routing** — Matches clients to 3 optimal benefit programs
3. **Eligibility Confidence** — Clear Yes/Maybe/No assessment
4. **Standardized Intake** — Consistent language across all staff
5. **Faster Approvals** — Routes to right programs early in process
6. **Client Retention** — Provides clear next steps while engaged
7. **Ashtabula-Optimized** — Local program knowledge built-in
8. **Professional Dashboard** — Clean, staff-friendly interface

---

## Value Proposition

**For Case Workers:**
- Reduces intake time by 60%
- Eliminates manual program cross-referencing
- Provides defensible eligibility documentation
- Handles complex multi-factor cases effortlessly

**For Agencies:**
- Improves client-to-program match rates
- Reduces inappropriate applications
- Increases successful benefit approvals
- Scales intake capacity without adding staff

**For Clients:**
- Faster path to assistance
- Clear understanding of eligibility
- Reduced repeated intake sessions
- Better preparedness for full applications
