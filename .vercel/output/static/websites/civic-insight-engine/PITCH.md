# Civic Insight Engine — Pitch Document

## One-Liner
AI-powered municipal transparency platform that makes government meetings, budgets, and community issues accessible to every citizen.

---

## 30-Second Demo Script

**Hook:** "Imagine you want to know what your city council decided last night about that new development project, but you don't have 3 hours to watch a boring meeting video..."

**Demo:**
1. **(2s)** Open newashtabula.vercel.app/civic-insight/
2. **(10s)** "Here's the AI Meeting Summarizer — I upload last night's council meeting PDF..."
3. **(10s)** "And in seconds, I get a clear summary of every decision, vote, and action item."
4. **(5s)** "I can also browse the transparency dashboard — property lookups, budget explorers, all in one place."
5. **(3s)** "Know what your government is doing, without the headache."

---

## What It Does
Transforms complex municipal data into citizen-friendly insights using AI summarization, interactive dashboards, and community issue tracking.

---

## Who It's For
- **Primary:** Engaged citizens who want to stay informed
- **Secondary:** Journalists, activists, local business owners
- **Tertiary:** Municipal staff seeking better public engagement

---

## The Problem
Government transparency data is:
- Scattered across multiple websites
- Buried in long PDFs and videos
- Written in bureaucratic language
- Hard to track over time

**Result:** Citizens are uninformed and disconnected from local government.

---

## The Solution
**Civic Insight Engine** brings everything together:
- 🤖 **AI Meeting Summaries** — Upload PDFs/videos, get instant insights
- 📍 **311 Issue Reporting** — Map-based community issue tracking
- 📊 **Transparency Dashboard** — Property, budget, and permit lookups
- 🔔 **Subscription Alerts** — Get notified about topics you care about

---

## Key Features
1. **AI-Powered Summarization** — Gemini API processes meetings in seconds
2. **Interactive Maps** — Visual issue reporting and tracking
3. **Unified Search** — One place to find any public record
4. **Mobile-First** — Works perfectly on phones
5. **PWA Support** — Install like a native app
