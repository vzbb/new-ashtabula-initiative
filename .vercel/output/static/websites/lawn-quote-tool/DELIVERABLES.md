## Phase 1: Research & Recon

### 2026-02-18 — Phase 1 Research Complete
**File:** `PHASE1-RESEARCH.md` (11.4KB)

**Key Findings:**
- 20-30 lawn care businesses in Ashtabula County mapped
- National competitors (LawnGuru, TaskEasy) charge 15-30% commission
- Local operators lack instant quote tools they can brand as their own
- Average Ohio lawn mowing: $53.32; per-acre: $85-150
- Peak quote season: March-April (ideal launch window)

**Personas Identified:**
- Solo Steve (one-person operation, low tech comfort)
- Growing Gary (2-3 crew owner, wants professional image)
- Customer Cathy (homeowner, wants instant answers)

**Differentiation:**
- Own your customers (vs. marketplace model)
- Local Ashtabula branding
- Simple setup vs. enterprise software complexity
- Affordable pricing vs. marketplace commissions

**Open Questions (5 blockers for Phase 2):**
1. Satellite imagery API cost viability
2. Pricing model validation (free vs. freemium vs. paid)
3. Contractor tech adoption barriers
4. Integration opportunities (service-scheduler, Stripe)
5. Snow removal extension potential
