## 4. Material Categories & Calculator Logic

### Material Types to Support

| Category | Common Types | Unit | Calculator |
|----------|--------------|------|------------|
| **Gravel** | #57, #411, #8, #9, pea gravel | tons | Area × depth × density |
| **Topsoil** | Screened, unscreened, garden mix | cubic yards | Area × depth |
| **Fill dirt** | Clean fill, structural fill | cubic yards | Area × depth |
| **Sand** | Mason sand, concrete sand | tons | Area × depth × density |
| **Crushed concrete** | Recycled base material | tons | Area × depth × density |
| **Mulch** | Hardwood, cedar, colored | cubic yards | Area × depth |
| **Stone** | Decorative, rip rap | tons | Area × depth × density |

### Conversion Factors

| Material | Tons per Cubic Yard | Notes |
|----------|---------------------|-------|
| Gravel | 1.4 | Varies by type |
| Topsoil | 1.0-1.3 | Moisture dependent |
| Sand | 1.3-1.5 | Varies by type |
| Stone | 1.5-1.8 | Depends on density |

### Calculator Inputs

**Basic Calculation:**
```
Area (sq ft) × Depth (inches) ÷ 12 = Cubic Feet
Cubic Feet ÷ 27 = Cubic Yards
Cubic Yards × Tons/CY = Tons Required
Add 10% for compaction/waste
```

**Project Types:**
- Driveway (gravel base + top layer)
- Patio base (gravel + sand)
- Landscaping bed (topsoil + mulch)
- Foundation backfill (fill dirt)
- Drainage (stone + gravel)

## 6. Revenue Model Options

### Option 1: Commission per Order (Recommended)
- Customers pay supplier pricing + 5-10% platform fee
- Suppliers get full material price
- Platform keeps transaction fee
- Example: $500 order → $25-50 platform fee

### Option 2: Supplier Subscription
- Suppliers pay monthly fee for unlimited quotes
- $99/month small supplier
- $199/month medium supplier
- $299/month large supplier
- No per-order fees

### Option 3: Hybrid
- Free tier: Commission-based (10%)
- Pro tier: $149/month (5% commission)
- Enterprise tier: $299/month (0% commission)
