## Typical Project Workflows

### New Home Construction
1. **City PCD:** Zoning Permit Application + Site Plan
2. **City PCD:** Approval (typically 7-10 days)
3. **County:** Residential Building Permit + Stamped Drawings
4. **County:** Trade permits (Electrical, Plumbing, HVAC)
5. **Construction:** With required inspections
6. **County:** Final inspection + Certificate of Occupancy

### Swimming Pool (In-Ground)
1. **City PCD:** Pool Permit Application + Site Plan
2. **City PCD:** Approval (5-7 days)
3. **County:** Building Permit (if structural) or approval note
4. **County:** Electrical Permit (pool equipment)
5. **Installation:** With required inspections
6. **Final:** Safety enclosure inspection

### Deck (Attached)
1. **City PCD:** Deck Permit Application + Site Plan/Drawings
2. **City PCD:** Approval (5-7 days)
3. **County:** Building Permit (if elevated/structural)
4. **Construction:** With footing and final inspections

### Fence
1. **City PCD:** Fence Permit Application + Site Plan
2. **City PCD:** Approval (3-5 days for simple cases)
3. **Installation:** No county permit required for standard fences

### Kitchen Remodel (Interior)
1. **County:** Residential Alteration Permit (if structural)
2. **County:** Electrical Permit (if new circuits)
3. **County:** Plumbing Permit (if moving fixtures)
4. **Work:** With required inspections

### Shed (<200 sq ft)
1. **City PCD:** May be exempt from zoning permit
2. Verify with PCD: (440) 992-7112
3. No county permit required for non-electrified sheds
