**Project:** permit-whisperer (New Ashtabula Initiative)  
**Created:** February 18, 2026  
**Status:** Research Complete — Ready for Implementation

---

## Executive Summary

Ashtabula County has a **fragmented, multi-jurisdictional zoning system** spanning 27 townships, 3 cities, and 1 village. Each jurisdiction maintains its own forms, processes, and contact points. This inventory maps all known permit types, form locations, and submission methods to inform the permit-whisperer knowledge base.

**Key Finding:** No single portal exists. Residents must navigate 30+ separate offices depending on project location.

## 1. Municipal Zoning (Cities)

### City of Ashtabula
**Zoning Authority:** Planning & Community Development (PCD)  
**Contact:** PCD@cityofashtabula.com  
**Phone:** (440) 992-7110  
**Fax:** (440) 992-7180  
**Office:** City Hall, 4717 Main Avenue

**Available Forms (PDF):**
| Form | Purpose | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|
| General Zoning Permit | Swimming pools, sheds, decks, garages | Standard residential improvements |
| Business Zoning Permit | New business establishment | No plumbing inspection required for existing buildings without structural changes |
| Certificate of Appropriateness | Historic district exterior work/signs | Reviewed by Architectural & Restoration Review Board |

**Process:**
1. Download/print PDF form
2. Fill out manually
3. Submit via: in-person, fax (440) 992-7180, or email PCD@cityofashtabula.com
4. Review period: up to 1 week
5. Pay fees at pickup (varies by permit type)

**Special Districts:**
- **Ashtabula Harbor Historical District:** Additional review required for signs/exterior work

## 2. County-Level Zoning

### Ashtabula County Building Department
**Address:** 25 W. Jefferson St., Jefferson, OH 44047  
**Phone:** (440) 576-6010 x3 (Health Dept for plumbing)  

**Process:**
1. Contact **local township zoning** for project approval
2. If plumbing involved: contact Health Department
3. Submit to Building Department:
   - Zoning approval copy
   - Plumbing approval (if applicable)
   - 4 sets of construction documents
   - Complete application

**Note:** Zoning approval must be obtained BEFORE building permit release

---

### Ashtabula County Planning & Development
**Role:** Reviews township zoning amendments and major subdivisions  
**Process:** Townships submit text/map amendments to County Planning for Commission review

## 4. Special Permit Types

### Residential Improvements (City of Ashtabula)
| Project | Permit Required | Special Notes |
|---------|----------------|---------------|
| Swimming Pool | Zoning Permit | Safety enclosure requirements |
| Shed/Garage | Zoning Permit | Setback compliance required |
| Deck | Zoning Permit | Height/location restrictions |
| Fence | Check with PCD | May not require permit |
| Demolition | ❌ Not issued by PCD | Contact appropriate department |

### Business Establishment
| Scenario | Requirements |
|----------|-------------|
| New Business (Existing Building) | Business Zoning Permit, NO plumbing inspection |
| New Business (New Construction) | Full zoning + building permits, plumbing inspection |
| Historic District Location | Certificate of Appropriateness + standard permits |

### Current State
| Jurisdiction | Digital Forms | Online Submission | Phone Support | Email Support |
|--------------|--------------|-------------------|---------------|---------------|

| Townships | ⚠️ Variable | ❌ Rarely | ⚠️ Variable | ⚠️ Variable |

### Key Gaps (permit-whisperer Opportunities)
1. **No unified portal** — 30+ separate entry points
2. **PDF-only forms** — No fillable online forms
3. **No status tracking** — Applicants must call/email for updates
4. **Inconsistent hours** — Township offices have limited availability
5. **No fee calculator** — Fee schedules not always published
