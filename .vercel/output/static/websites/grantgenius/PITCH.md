## 30-Second Demo Script

### Hook
> "Imagine you're a nonprofit director trying to win a critical grant, but you're staring at a blank page because you don't know how to align your mission with the funder's requirements..."

### Demo Steps (30 seconds total)

**Step 1 (5 sec):** Show the hero — "GrantGenius — AI Drafts in Minutes"

**Step 2 (6 sec):** Paste a grant URL into the input field — any funder's RFP page

**Step 3 (6 sec):** Enter your organization's mission statement in the text area

**Step 4 (6 sec):** Click 'Generate Draft' — AI analyzes the grant and creates a custom outline

**Step 5 (7 sec):** Show the generated output — 5 sections: Need, Goals, Approach, Budget Rationale, Impact — ready to export

## Key Features

1. **URL-to-Draft** — Paste any grant URL, AI extracts requirements and expectations
2. **Mission Alignment** — Integrates your organization's mission into every section
3. **5-Section Structure** — Generates Need, Goals, Approach, Budget Rationale, and Impact sections
4. **200-Word Executive Draft** — Concise, funder-ready narrative outline
5. **Gemini-Powered** — Uses Google's Gemini 1.5 Flash for fast, accurate generation
6. **Export Ready** — Copy to clipboard or download for sharing with stakeholders
7. **Rapid Turnaround** — Draft generated in seconds, not days
8. **Board-Ready Copy** — Professional language suitable for executive review

## Technical Notes

- **Stack:** React + Vite + Google Gemini API
- **Input:** Grant URL (any web-accessible RFP) + Mission statement
