# Boat Storage Waitlist — Pitch Document

## 30-Second Demo Script

"Imagine you're running a marina, and it's Labor Day weekend. Your phone is blowing up with boat owners calling about winter storage. You're trying to take down names, boat sizes, and contact info on a notepad while also handling dock traffic. Some calls you miss completely. Some inquiries you forget to follow up on.

Watch this. A boat owner fills in their details—22-foot boat—and clicks Generate Confirmation. Instantly, they get a professional confirmation message that includes an estimated timing window and a subtle upsell for spring launch service.

The customer feels taken care of immediately, you capture their commitment, and you've planted the seed for an additional $200 service in the spring. That's the Boat Storage Waitlist—filling your winter storage and maximizing lifetime customer value."

---

## What/Who/CTA

**What it is:** An AI-powered system that generates instant waitlist confirmations with timing estimates and upsell opportunities.

**Who it's for:** Marina operators, boatyard managers, and storage facility owners in Ashtabula County preparing for winter storage season.

**Call to Action:** "Let's get this live before Labor Day weekend so you stop losing storage deposits to the competition."

---

## Key Features

1. **Instant Confirmations** — Professional waitlist responses in 5 seconds
2. **Timing Estimates** — Automatic inclusion of expected storage windows
3. **Built-In Upsells** — Spring launch and maintenance packages promoted naturally
4. **Recurring Revenue Focus** — Converts one-time storage into year-round relationships
5. **Drop-Off Prevention** — Fast response reduces the chance customers book elsewhere
6. **Contact Capture** — Collects phone/email for follow-up marketing

---

## Value Proposition

For marinas competing for limited winter storage spots, this tool ensures every inquiry gets an immediate, professional response—capturing more deposits while automatically promoting high-margin spring services.

---

## Demo Steps

1. **Set the Scene** — "Labor Day weekend, marina is packed, phone ringing..."
2. **Enter Details** — Input boat length and contact info
3. **Generate** — Click "Generate Confirmation" button
4. **Review Output** — Show confirmation with timing and spring upsell
5. **Close** — "Customer's happy, deposit's secured, spring service booked"

---

## Technical Notes

- **API:** Google Gemini 1.5 Flash
- **Setup:** VITE_GEMINI_API_KEY environment variable required
- **No data storage** — Customer info processed via API, not stored locally
