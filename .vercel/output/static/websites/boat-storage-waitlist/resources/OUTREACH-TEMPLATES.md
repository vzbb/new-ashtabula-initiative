## Phone Script (Cold Call)

### Opening (30 seconds)
> "Hi, this is [NAME] from Noirsys, a local tech company here in Ashtabula County. We're building tools specifically for marinas to help with winter storage waitlist management. Do you have 2 minutes? I'd love to learn about how you currently handle storage requests."

### Discovery Questions
1. "How do you currently manage your waitlist for winter boat storage?"
2. "What's the biggest pain point during busy season — August through October?"
3. "How quickly do you typically respond to storage inquiries?"
4. "Do you ever lose customers because of slow response times?"
5. "What upsells do you offer — spring launch, maintenance, detailing?"

### Value Proposition (If Interested)
> "We've built a tool that automatically confirms waitlist signups via SMS/email with personalized messages that include upsell suggestions for spring services. It reduces your admin time and captures revenue you might be missing. Would you be open to a 15-minute demo?"

### Close
> "I'll send you a quick email with my contact info and a link to see what we're building. What's the best email for you?"
