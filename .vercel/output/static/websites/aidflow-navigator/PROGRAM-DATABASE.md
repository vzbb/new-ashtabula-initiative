## Program 1: SNAP (Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program)

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Category** | Food |
| **Administered By** | Ohio Department of Job & Family Services (ODJFS) |
| **Phone** | (866) 244-0071 |
| **Eligibility** | Based on gross and net income, household size |
| **Income Limits (2025)** | Varies by household size (federal poverty guidelines) |
| **Who Qualifies** | Low-income individuals and families |
| **Benefits** | Monthly food assistance via EBT card |
| **Application** | Online, by phone, or in person at county office |
| **Documentation** | ID, income proof, residency, expenses |
| **Auto-Qualifies For** | WIC (if income eligible) |

## Program 2: WIC (Women, Infants, and Children)

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Category** | Food/Nutrition |
| **Administered By** | Ashtabula County Community Action Agency (ACCAA) |
| **Phone** | Check accaa.org for current number |
| **Eligibility** | Income + categorical requirements |
| **Who Qualifies** | Pregnant women, breastfeeding women (up to 1 year postpartum), non-breastfeeding women (up to 6 months postpartum), infants, children up to age 5 |
| **Income Limit** | 185% of federal poverty level |
| **Auto-Qualifies** | If receiving SNAP, Medicaid, or TANF |
| **Benefits** | Food vouchers, nutrition education, breastfeeding support |
| **Note** | Pregnant woman counts as 2+ household members |

## Program 3: HEAP (Home Energy Assistance Program)

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Category** | Energy/Utilities |
| **Administered By** | Ohio Development Services Agency |
| **Program Year** | July 2024 – May 2025 |
| **Eligibility** | Income eligible Ohioans |
| **Benefits** | One-time payment toward heating bills |
| **Winter Crisis Program** | Emergency assistance for disconnection threats, disconnection, new service establishment |
| **PIPP (Percentage of Income Payment Plan Plus)** | Helps manage ongoing utility costs |
| **Application** | Online or through local Community Action Agency |
| **Documentation** | Income, utility bills, ID |

## Program 4: Housing Choice Voucher (Section 8)

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Category** | Housing |
| **Administered By** | Local Public Housing Agencies (PHAs) |
| **Eligibility** | Low-income families, elderly, disabled, veterans |
| **How It Works** | Subsidy paid directly to landlord; tenant pays difference |
| **Waitlist** | Typically long; check with local PHA for status |
| **Housing Types** | Single-family homes, townhouses, apartments |
| **Application** | Through local PHA (varies by county) |
| **Note** | Waitlists often closed; check periodically |
