## Initial Findings

### Ashtabula Economic Development Landscape

**Key Organizations:**
- Ashtabula County Economic Development (ACED)
- Growth Partnership for Ashtabula County
- Ohio Southeast Economic Development (OhioSE)
- JobsOhio (statewide)

**Major Assets:**
- Port of Ashtabula (Great Lakes port)
- Norfolk Southern rail access
- I-90 corridor access
- Northeast Ohio regional proximity

**Data Sources Identified:**
