## Executive Summary

Analyzed 4 comparable economic development organizations to identify best practices, feature gaps, and differentiation opportunities for invest-ashtabula. Key finding: **Most regional ED sites lack interactive tools** — presenting an opportunity for Ashtabula to leapfrog with utility capacity dashboards and AI-powered site matching.

## Best Practices Identified

### 1. Lead with Data (But Keep It Simple)
- JobsOhio: 5 key stats above fold
- Columbus Region: "By the Numbers" grid
- **Takeaway:** Lead with 3-5 compelling data points, not paragraphs

### 2. Social Proof Builds Trust
- All successful sites feature testimonials from recognizable names
- Anduril, Honda, Forge Biologics logos = instant credibility
- **Takeaway:** Collect 3-5 local success stories with quotes + photos

### 3. Consultation is the Conversion Goal
- No competitor offers fully self-serve site selection
- Contact forms are multi-step to qualify leads
- **Takeaway:** Design for "book a consultation" not "download brochure"

### 4. Content Marketing Drives SEO
- Columbus Region: Regular news updates on business wins
- Team NEO: Annual impact reports
- **Takeaway:** Plan content calendar for success stories, infrastructure updates

### 5. Utility Data is the Differentiator
- No competitor shows real-time utility capacity
- All require phone calls for infrastructure details
- **Takeaway:** This is Ashtabula's wedge — invest in utility dashboard

## Differentiation Strategy for invest-ashtabula

### 1. The "Utility Transparency" Angle
**Positioning:** "Know before you call" — See real utility capacity, not just site outlines.

**Implementation:**
- Color-coded utility zones (green = available capacity)
- Click-through to detailed specs (kVA available, water GPM, gas pressure)
- Compare multiple sites side-by-side

### 2. The "AI Matchmaker" Feature
**Positioning:** "Tell us what you need, we'll find where it fits."

**Implementation:**
- Natural language input: "I need 50K sq ft with rail access and 480V 3-phase"
- Matching algorithm scores sites by fit percentage
- Explains why each site matches (transparency)

### 3. The "No Games" Transparency
**Positioning:** Real pricing, real timelines, real requirements.

**Implementation:**
- Display estimated lease/purchase ranges
- Show typical permitting timelines
- List requirements upfront (no "contact us for details")

### 4. The "Community Context" Layer
**Positioning:** See the community, not just the site.

**Implementation:**
- Amenities overlay (dining, housing, schools)
- Commute time visualizations
- Local workforce training programs

## Technical Architecture Insights

### From JobsOhio
- Uses Site Selection Plus (commercial platform)
- Heavy investment in aerial/satellite imagery
- CRM integration for lead tracking

### From Columbus Region
- Content management for news/blog
- Event integration (job fairs, ribbon cuttings)
- Newsletter signup prominent

### From Team NEO
- PDF report generation (annual impact)
- Research database with historical trends
- Service taxonomy with clear CTAs

**Recommended Stack for invest-ashtabula:**
- **Map:** Mapbox GL JS (matches SPEC.md)
- **CMS:** Supabase (database + auth + storage)
- **Site data:** PostGIS-enabled PostgreSQL
- **Forms:** Calendly + custom Supabase forms
- **Hosting:** Vercel (Next.js)
