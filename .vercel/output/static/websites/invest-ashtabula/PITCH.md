# Invest Ashtabula — Pitch Document

## One-Liner
A modern investment attraction platform showcasing Ashtabula's opportunities with interactive data visualizations and streamlined business engagement.

---

## 30-Second Demo Script

**Hook:** "You're a business owner looking to expand, and you've heard about this up-and-coming area called Ashtabula — but where do you start?"

**Demo:**
1. **(2s)** Open newashtabula.vercel.app/invest/
2. **(10s)** "Here's the Investment Dashboard — I can see population trends, economic growth, workforce data, all visualized beautifully."
3. **(10s)** "Let me drill down into the port facilities — shipping volume, infrastructure, available sites..."
4. **(5s)** "And when I'm ready, I can submit an inquiry right here."
5. **(3s)** "All the data you need, none of the runaround."

---

## What It Does
Showcases regional investment opportunities through compelling data visualization, site directories, and direct business engagement tools.

---

## Who It's For
- **Primary:** Business owners seeking expansion locations
- **Secondary:** Site selectors, real estate developers
- **Tertiary:** Economic development professionals

---

## The Problem
Economic development websites are typically:
- Outdated and clunky
- Buried in spreadsheets
- Lacking visual appeal
- Hard to navigate

**Result:** Missed investment opportunities and frustrated prospects.

---

## The Solution
**Invest Ashtabula** makes the case visually:
- 📈 **Economic Dashboards** — Trends, demographics, workforce data
- 🏭 **Site Directories** — Available properties with detailed specs
- 🚢 **Infrastructure Maps** — Port, rail, highway connectivity
- 📋 **Inquiry Forms** — Streamlined business engagement

---

## Key Features
1. **Interactive Data Viz** — Charts, graphs, maps
2. **Property Database** — Searchable with filters
3. **Workforce Insights** — Skills, wages, education
4. **Infrastructure Details** — Transportation, utilities
5. **Lead Generation** — Built-in contact forms

---

## Call to Action
**For Economic Dev:** "Showcase your region's potential with data that convinces."

**For Investors:** "Discover why Ashtabula is the smart choice for your next expansion."
