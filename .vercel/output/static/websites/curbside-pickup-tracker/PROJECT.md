# PROJECT — Curbside Pickup Tracker

## Vision
A lightweight, SMS-based curbside pickup coordination tool specifically designed for small-town independent businesses in Ashtabula County that cannot justify expensive enterprise solutions.

## Problem Statement
Independent restaurants and retailers in Ashtabula lack affordable, easy-to-implement curbside pickup solutions. National chains (Walmart, Target, ALDI) offer sophisticated curbside, but existing solutions for small businesses require:
- Customer app downloads (SWIPEBY)
- High monthly fees ($79-500/month)
- Complex POS integrations
- Technical expertise small businesses don't have

## Target Users
- **Primary:** Independent restaurants (25+ in GOTL, 100+ county-wide)
- **Secondary:** Local retailers, specialty shops
- **Tertiary:** Seasonal tourism businesses

## Value Proposition
"The curbside pickup tool designed for small-town businesses — no apps, no complex setup, just simple SMS coordination that your customers already know how to use."

## Competitive Positioning
| Competitor | Their Price | Our Advantage |
|------------|-------------|---------------|
| SWIPEBY | $58/month | No customer app required |
| SimpleTexting | $29/month | Curbside-specific features |
| Curbspot | $79/month | Lower price, simpler setup |
| DoorDash | 15-30% | You keep the customer relationship |

## Market Opportunity
- BOPIS market: $140 billion (2024)
- Ashtabula County: 100-150 restaurants, 200+ Chamber businesses
- Gap: No solution designed specifically for small-town independent businesses
