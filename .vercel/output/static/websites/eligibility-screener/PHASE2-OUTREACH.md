## 2. Outreach Email Templates

### Template A: ACDJFS Partnership Introduction

**Subject:** Free Digital Tool to Help Residents Find Benefits — Partnership Opportunity

```
Dear [Director's Name],

I'm reaching out on behalf of a local initiative to improve access to social services in Ashtabula County.

We're building eligibility-screener.com — a free, mobile-friendly screening tool that helps residents understand which programs they may qualify for (SNAP, Medicaid, childcare subsidies, OWF) before they apply.

**Why this matters:** Many eligible residents don't apply due to confusion about requirements. A simple wizard could increase application rates and reduce incomplete applications.

**What we're requesting:**
1. 15-minute call to verify program details and eligibility rules
2. Permission to link directly to Ohio Benefits portal
3. Feedback on our eligibility logic to ensure accuracy
4. Optional: endorsement as a community resource

**What's in it for ACDJFS:**
- Fewer calls from residents asking "Do I qualify?"
- More complete applications with proper documentation
- Reaches residents who avoid phone/visit due to anxiety

**About us:** This is a community service project with no advertising, no data sales, and no fees. We're seeking accuracy and community impact, not revenue.

Would you be available for a brief call next week? I'm happy to work around your schedule.

Best regards,
[Name]
[Contact]
[Project background link if available]
```

## 6. Competitive Feature Audit

### FindHelp.org Analysis
- **Strengths:** National coverage, clean interface, search by need
- **Gaps:** No eligibility wizard, not Ashtabula-specific, no Spanish
- **Opportunity:** Local focus + eligibility guidance + multilingual

### Benefits.gov Analysis
- **Strengths:** Official government source, comprehensive
- **Gaps:** Dated UI, no wizard, overwhelming options
- **Opportunity:** Modern, mobile-first, simplified flow

### 211 Analysis
- **Strengths:** Human support, comprehensive, trusted
- **Gaps:** Phone-only for many, wait times, no digital self-service
- **Opportunity:** Digital complement to 211, not replacement

### Ohio Benefits Portal
- **Strengths:** Direct application pathway, official source
- **Gaps:** Complex for first-time users, no pre-screening
- **Opportunity:** Pre-screening wizard that feeds into portal
