## 4. Key Questions to Ask During Outreach

### For Restaurant Managers/Owners
1. How do diners currently get wine pairing advice?
2. What are the biggest pain points with your wine service?
3. Would you pay $49/month for a tool that increased wine sales by 10-15%?
4. Who maintains your wine list currently?
5. What's your biggest hesitation about QR-code dining tools?

### For Servers
1. How often do diners ask for wine recommendations?
2. Do you feel confident making pairing suggestions?
3. Would a QR tool help you focus on other service tasks?
4. What questions do diners ask most about wine?

### For Diners (Survey)
1. When dining at a winery restaurant, do you struggle choosing wine?
2. Would you use a free QR-code tool for pairing suggestions?
3. What would make you trust (or distrust) an AI wine recommendation?
