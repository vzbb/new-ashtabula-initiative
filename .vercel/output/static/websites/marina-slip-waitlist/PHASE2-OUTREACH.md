## 2. Phone Call Script

### Opening (30 seconds)
> "Hi, this is [NAME] from a local tech initiative supporting Ashtabula County marinas. We're building tools to help marinas manage slip waitlists more efficiently. Do you have a few minutes to share how you currently handle waitlists?"

### Discovery Questions
1. **Current process:** "How do you manage your waitlist for seasonal slips? Paper, spreadsheet, software, or something else?"
2. **Volume:** "How many people are typically on your waitlist at any given time?"
3. **Pain points:** "What's the biggest frustration with your current waitlist system?"
4. **Turnover:** "How often do slips open up unexpectedly mid-season?"
5. **Communication:** "When a slip opens, how do you contact the next person? How long does that take?"
6. **No-shows:** "Have you had slips sit empty because you couldn't reach someone on the waitlist quickly enough?"
7. **Pricing interest:** "Would you pay $49-99/month for a digital waitlist that automatically notifies boaters, tracks position, and fills slips faster?"

### Closing
> "Thanks so much for your time. We're building a pilot program with 2-3 local marinas this spring. Would you be interested in a free trial once it's ready? I can send you a one-page overview via email."

## 6. Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We don't have a waitlist problem" | "That's great! Many marinas tell us the same until they realize how many hours staff spend answering 'where am I on the list?' calls. Would you be open to a 5-minute conversation to confirm?" |
| "We already use Dockwa/Snag-A-Slip" | "Excellent — those are great for transient bookings. Are you also using them for your seasonal waitlist, or is that managed separately?" |
| "We don't have budget for software" | "Completely understand. That's why we're offering the pilot season free — we want to prove the value first. If it doesn't save you time or fill slips faster, you pay nothing." |
| "We're too small to need software" | "Actually, smaller marinas often benefit most — you don't have dedicated office staff, so every hour saved on admin is huge. Our pricing scales to your size." |
| "Our boaters aren't tech-savvy" | "Good point. The tool is designed so boaters don't need to download anything — they just get text/email updates. But we'd love your input on making it accessible for your customers." |
