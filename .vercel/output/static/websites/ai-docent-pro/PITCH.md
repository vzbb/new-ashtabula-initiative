# AI Docent Pro — Pitch Document

## 30-Second Demo Script

"Imagine you're the curator at the Ashtabula Maritime Museum, and you've just received a donation of copper tools that harbor workers used in the 1920s. They're fascinating artifacts, but you need a compelling narration for visitors—and you need exhibit tags, and you need it all by next week's board meeting.

Watch this. I describe the artifacts—'copper tools used by harbor workers'—and click Generate Narration. In seconds, I have a 120-word docent script that brings these tools to life, plus 5 curated tags for cataloging. The tone is perfect for visitors, the facts are grounded in local history, and it's ready to print for your volunteer docents.

Instead of spending your weekend writing copy, you review, tweak, and publish. That's AI Docent Pro—turning artifacts into unforgettable stories."

---

## What/Who/CTA

**What it is:** An AI-powered tool that generates museum-quality narration, exhibit tags, and metadata for artifacts and displays.

**Who it's for:** Museum curators, historical society volunteers, gallery owners, and cultural institutions in Ashtabula County.

**Call to Action:** "Bring your most interesting artifact next week and let's create narration that makes visitors stop and really look."

---

## Key Features

1. **Museum-Quality Narration** — 120-word scripts optimized for visitor engagement
2. **Automatic Tagging** — Generates 5 relevant exhibit tags for cataloging
3. **Audio-Ready Scripts** — Natural flow designed for spoken delivery
4. **Consistent Tone** — Maintains institutional voice across all exhibits
5. **Accessibility Support** — Instant audio narration for diverse visitor needs
6. **Gemini AI Powered** — Context-aware storytelling that respects historical accuracy

---

## Value Proposition

For small museums with big stories but limited staff time, AI Docent Pro transforms artifact research and narration from a weekend-consuming task into a 5-second process—allowing curators to focus on acquisition and preservation.

---

## Demo Steps

1. **Set the Scene** — "New donation arrived—copper tools from the 1920s harbor..."
2. **Describe Artifact** — Enter description in the text area
3. **Generate** — Click "Generate Narration" button
4. **Review Output** — Show the narration and tags
5. **Close** — "Ready for your docents by Monday, board presentation ready"

---

## Technical Notes

- **API:** Google Gemini 1.5 Flash
- **Setup:** VITE_GEMINI_API_KEY environment variable required
- **No data storage** — Artifact descriptions processed via API, not retained
