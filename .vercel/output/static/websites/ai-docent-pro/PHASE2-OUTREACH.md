## 3. Interview Questions for Discovery Calls

### For Museum Leadership
1. How many visitors do you see annually? Peak vs. off-season?
2. What's your current volunteer docent capacity?
3. Have you looked into audio guides before? What stopped you?
4. What's your annual technology budget (roughly)?
5. What are your top 3 pain points with visitor experience?

### For Curators/Educators
1. Which 20–30 artifacts would you most want visitors to understand deeply?
2. Do you have existing written descriptions we could adapt?
3. What's the most common question visitors ask?
4. How do you verify historical accuracy of information?
5. Any artifacts with particularly compelling stories?

### For Visitor Experience
1. What demographics visit most? (seniors, families, schools, tourists)
2. Do visitors typically read placards, or walk past them?
3. Have you surveyed visitors about what they want more of?
4. Any accessibility needs we should prioritize? (hearing, vision, mobility)
5. What's the typical visit duration?

### For Technical Infrastructure
1. Is there WiFi throughout the museum?
2. How's cell reception inside the building?
3. Do you have someone who manages technology, or is it volunteer-led?
4. Any existing digital asset management (photos, descriptions database)?
5. Are you comfortable with cloud-based services, or prefer local-only?
