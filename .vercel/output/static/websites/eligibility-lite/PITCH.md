# Eligibility Lite — Pitch Materials

## 30-Second Demo Script

**HOOK:**
"Imagine you're a case worker at a community agency—and you have 20 families waiting, phones ringing, and only two hours to screen everyone for benefits eligibility."

**DEMO (5 steps, ~30 seconds):**

1. *Enter "4"* for household size
2. *Type "1800"* for monthly income
3. *Click "Run Screener"*
4. *AI analyzes in 5 seconds:* income against regional guidelines
5. *Clear result appears:* likely eligibility status + 2 recommended programs to pursue

**WHAT IT DOES:**
AI-powered eligibility pre-screener that instantly evaluates household criteria and matches families to relevant benefit programs in 60 seconds.

**WHO IT'S FOR:**
Community action agencies, social services departments, and nonprofits doing initial benefits intake and screening.

**CTA:**
"Let's set up a pilot with your intake team—most agencies see 60% faster screening times within the first week."

---

## Key Features

1. **60-Second Screening** — Fast household pre-qualification
2. **Dual Input Fields** — Household size + monthly income
3. **Program Matching** — AI suggests 2 most relevant benefit programs
4. **Instant AI Analysis** — 5-second response via Gemini API
5. **Clear Eligibility Language** — Reduces confusion for clients
6. **Drop-off Reduction** — Keeps clients engaged with quick results
7. **Staff Efficiency Tool** — Less manual calculation and research
8. **ACCAA Branded** — Ashtabula County Community Action Agency

---

## Value Proposition

**For Agencies:**
- Screens 3x more clients per hour
- Reduces intake backlog and wait times
- Standardizes eligibility language across staff
- Improves data quality for downstream applications

**For Clients:**
- Knows eligibility prospects immediately
- Gets clear next steps while motivated
- Reduces anxiety about application outcomes
- Spends less time in waiting rooms
