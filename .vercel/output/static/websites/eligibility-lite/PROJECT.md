# Eligibility Lite

**Status:** 🟡 Phase 1 Complete  
**Priority:** P2 (Civic/Social Services)  
**Target:** Ashtabula County residents needing benefits screening

---

## Problem
Complex eligibility rules confuse residents. Many don't know which benefits they qualify for (SNAP, Medicaid, utility assistance). Missed benefits due to lack of awareness.

## Solution
5-minute anonymous questionnaire tells residents which programs they likely qualify for and next steps.

## Market
- 97,000 population
- 17-20% poverty rate
- 15,000-18,000 SNAP recipients
- 5,000-8,000 Medicaid eligible but unenrolled

## Programs
SNAP, Medicaid, HEAP, TANF, WIC, housing vouchers

## Revenue
Free civic tool. Fund via county sponsorship, foundation grants, hospital community benefit.
