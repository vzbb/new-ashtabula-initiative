# Eligibility Lite — Phase 1 Research
## Simplified Benefits Screening for Ashtabula County Residents

**Date:** February 19, 2026  
**Status:** Phase 1 Research  
**Next:** Phase 2 Resource Procurement

---

## 1. Problem Statement

**Pain Points:**
- Residents don't know which benefits they qualify for (SNAP, Medicaid, utility assistance, etc.)
- Complex eligibility rules are confusing
- Multiple applications with duplicate information
- Missed benefits due to lack of awareness
- Stigma prevents some from seeking help

---

## 2. Market Analysis

**Ashtabula County:**
- **Population:** 97,000
- **Poverty rate:** 17-20% (above state average)
- **SNAP recipients:** 15,000-18,000
- **Medicaid eligible but unenrolled:** Estimated 5,000-8,000
- **Utility assistance needs:** High (winter heating costs)

**Programs to Screen:**
| Program | Administered By | Eligibility |
|---------|-----------------|-------------|
| SNAP (Food assistance) | ACDJFS | Income < 130% FPL |
| Medicaid | ACDJFS | Income < 138% FPL |
| HEAP (Utility) | ACCAA | Income < 175% FPL |
| TANF (Cash) | ACDJFS | Very low income |
| WIC | Health Dept | Pregnant/postpartum + children |
| Housing vouchers | AMHA | Income < 50% AMI |

---

## 3. Competitor Analysis

| Platform | Approach | Limitation |
|----------|----------|------------|
| **Benefits.gov** | Federal | Complex, not local |
| **211 Helpline** | Phone-based | Wait times, limited hours |
| **ACDJFS office** | In-person | Intimidating, time-consuming |
| **Nonprofit screening** | Case by case | Limited capacity |

**Gap:** No simple, anonymous, mobile-friendly local screening tool.

---

## 4. Solution Concept

**Simple 5-minute questionnaire that tells residents which programs they likely qualify for and next steps.**

**Features:**
- Anonymous screening (no login required)
- Mobile-first design
- Plain language (no jargon)
- Local resource links
- Multiple program check at once
- Printable results

**Not:** Application submission, just screening and guidance.

---

## 5. Tech Stack

- Next.js + Tailwind
- Supabase (anonymous analytics)
- No authentication required
