# Artist Commission Form — Pitch Document

## 30-Second Demo Script

"Imagine you're a local artist, and you just got an inquiry for a mural commission. You're excited, but you're also in the middle of another project, and you know from experience that if you don't respond quickly, that potential client moves on.

Watch this. I enter the project details—style is Mural, budget is $500—and click Generate Reply. In 5 seconds, I have a professional, friendly response that acknowledges their request, shows enthusiasm for the project, and includes a clear next step to schedule a consultation.

Instead of stressing about what to write or letting it sit in your inbox, you send this immediately. Higher response rates mean more booked commissions. That's the Artist Commission Form—converting inquiries into paid work."

---

## What/Who/CTA

**What it is:** An AI-powered assistant that drafts professional commission responses for artists, including consultation CTAs.

**Who it's for:** Local artists, muralists, portrait painters, sculptors, and craftspeople who take commissions in Ashtabula County.

**Call to Action:** "Let me set this up on your phone so you can respond to inquiries in seconds, even when you're in the studio."

---

## Key Features

1. **Instant Professional Replies** — Generate context-aware responses in 5 seconds
2. **Consultation CTA Built-In** — Every reply includes next steps to move toward booking
3. **Customizable by Style/Budget** — Adapts tone and content based on project type and price
4. **Conversion Optimized** — Reduces response time, which dramatically increases close rates
5. **Gemini AI Powered** — Natural, warm language that sounds like you, not a robot
6. **Mobile-Friendly** — Respond to inquiries from anywhere, even between brushstrokes

---

## Value Proposition

For artists juggling creation with client communication, this tool eliminates the friction of responding to inquiries—turning more "maybes" into booked commissions while you stay focused on your art.

---

## Demo Steps

1. **Set the Scene** — "You're at your easel, phone buzzes with a mural inquiry..."
2. **Enter Details** — Input style and budget in the project card
3. **Generate** — Click "Generate Reply" button
4. **Review Output** — Show the professional, friendly response with consult CTA
5. **Close** — "Hit send, get back to painting, book the client"

---

## Technical Notes

- **API:** Google Gemini 1.5 Flash
- **Setup:** VITE_GEMINI_API_KEY environment variable required
- **No data storage** — Inquiries processed via API, no client data retained
