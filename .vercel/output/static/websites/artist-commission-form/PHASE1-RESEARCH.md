## 1. Problem Statement

### The Pain Point
Local artists in Ashtabula County struggle to find commissioned work:
- **Limited visibility:** Hard to reach potential buyers beyond word-of-mouth
- **No portfolio platform:** No central place to showcase work and availability
- **Pricing uncertainty:** Difficult to know what to charge, no market standards
- **Commission logistics:** Coordinating details, deposits, delivery is disorganized
- **Payment friction:** No easy way to accept payments or manage installments

### Impact on Buyers
- **Hard to find artists:** Don't know where to look for local talent
- **Portfolio gaps:** Can't easily browse styles and availability
- **Trust concerns:** Unclear pricing, no reviews, no contracts
- **Project coordination:** Difficult to communicate vision and timeline

## 3. Competitor Analysis

### National Artist Platforms

| Platform | Model | Cost | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|----------|-------|------|-----------|------------|
| **Etsy** | Marketplace | $0.20/listing + 6.5% | Traffic, established | Not commission-focused, fees |
| **Saatchi Art** | Gallery + sales | 35% commission | Curated, professional | High commission, selective |
| **Artfinder** | Gallery | 33% commission | International reach | High commission |
| **Fiverr** | Gig marketplace | 20% commission | Easy setup, price competition | Race to bottom |
| **Upwork** | Freelance | 5-20% commission | Professional projects | Not art-specific |
| **Instagram** | Social | Free | Visual platform | No transaction support |

### Local/Regional Options
- **Ashtabula Arts Center** — Physical gallery, limited reach
- **Local craft fairs** — Seasonal, time-intensive
- **Word of mouth** — Unpredictable, limited
- **Facebook Marketplace** — Not art-focused

### What Exists Locally
- **Nothing digital** — No Ashtabula-specific artist commission platform
- Artists currently use:
  - Social media (Instagram, Facebook)
  - Word of mouth
  - Gallery consignment (high commission)
  - Craft fairs (seasonal)

### Gap Analysis
**✓ Opportunity Confirmed:**
- No local platform for commission-based art
- National platforms take 20-35% commission
- No system for coordinating commission details
- No local artist discovery for tourists/residents

## 5. Solution Concept

### Core Concept
**"Fiverr meets Etsy for Ashtabula artists — local commission platform with built-in project management and payment protection."**

### Key Features

**For Artists:**
1. **Portfolio Pages** — Showcase work, bio, specialties, availability
2. **Commission Types** — Define services (portrait, landscape, pet, abstract, etc.)
3. **Pricing Guidance** — Suggested rates based on size, medium, complexity
4. **Custom Quotes** — Request form for unique projects
5. **Project Dashboard** — Track commissions, milestones, communication
6. **Payment Processing** — Deposits, milestones, final payment
7. **Calendar Integration** — Show availability, block project time
8. **Review System** — Build reputation

**For Buyers:**
1. **Artist Discovery** — Browse by medium, style, price range
2. **Portfolio Viewing** — High-quality image galleries
3. **Commission Request** — Structured form (size, timeline, budget, vision)
4. **Secure Payment** — Escrow/milestone payments
5. **Project Tracking** — See progress, communicate with artist
6. **Review System** — Rate completed commissions
7. **Local Pickup or Shipping** — Delivery options

**For Platform:**
1. **Artist Vetting** — Basic quality check (portfolio review)
2. **Dispute Resolution** — Mediation for conflicts
3. **Marketing** — Promote local artists, seasonal campaigns
4. **Business Services** — Corporate art procurement

### Differentiation

| Feature | Artist Commission | Etsy | Saatchi | Instagram |
|---------|-------------------|------|---------|-----------|
| Commission-focused | ✓ | ✗ | Partial | ✗ |
| Local Ashtabula | ✓ | ✗ | ✗ | Partial |
| Project management | ✓ | ✗ | ✗ | ✗ |
| Payment protection | ✓ | Partial | ✓ | ✗ |
| Lower commission | ✓ (10-15%) | 6.5%+ | 35% | Free (no sales) |
| Artist tools | ✓ | ✗ | ✗ | ✗ |

## 6. Tech Stack Options

### Recommended: Next.js + Supabase + Stripe
- **Frontend:** Next.js 14, Tailwind CSS
- **Database:** Supabase (PostgreSQL)
- **Payments:** Stripe Connect (split payments artist/platform)

- **Hosting:** Vercel

## 7. Revenue Model

### Commission-Based (Primary)
| Transaction Type | Platform Fee | Artist Receives |
|------------------|--------------|-----------------|
| Standard commission | 15% | 85% |
| Featured/promoted | 20% | 80% |
| Large projects ($2K+) | 10% | 90% |

### Additional Revenue
| Service | Price |
|---------|-------|
| **Featured listing** | $10-25/mo |
| **Priority placement** | $5-15/mo |
| **Website/portfolio** | $5-10/mo custom domain |
| **Marketing boosts** | $20-50/campaign |

### Example Economics ($500 commission):**
- Commission: $500
- Platform fee (15%): $75
- Artist receives: $425
- Payment processing (~3%): $15
- **Net platform revenue: $60**

### Scale Targets:**
- 20 commissions/month @ $500 avg = $1,200/mo revenue
- 50 commissions/month = $3,000/mo
- 100 commissions/month = $6,000/mo
