# Artist Commission Form

**Status:** 🟡 Phase 1 Complete (Research)  
**Priority:** P3 (Arts & Community)  
**Target:** Local artists and art buyers in Ashtabula County

---

## Problem
Local artists struggle to find commissioned work. Buyers don't know where to find artists. No platform for coordinating commission details, pricing, or payments.

## Solution
"Fiverr meets Etsy for Ashtabula artists" — local commission platform with portfolio showcase, project management, and payment protection.

## Key Differentiators
- Commission-focused (not just selling existing work)
- Local Ashtabula artist discovery
- Project management tools for commissions
- Payment protection (milestones, escrow)
- Lower fees than Saatchi (15% vs. 35%)

## Market
- 30-50 professional artists in Ashtabula County
- 200-400 hobbyist artists
- 10,000+ potential buyers (homeowners, businesses, tourists)
- Geneva-on-the-Lake: 500K+ annual tourists
- $200K-$500K annual commission potential

## Revenue Model
| Transaction | Platform Fee | Artist Receives |
|-------------|--------------|-----------------|
| Standard commission | 15% | 85% |
| Featured/promoted | 20% | 80% |
| Large projects ($2K+) | 10% | 90% |

## Tech Stack (Proposed)
- Next.js + Tailwind CSS
- Supabase (database + storage)
- Stripe Connect (split payments)
- Cloudinary (image optimization)
