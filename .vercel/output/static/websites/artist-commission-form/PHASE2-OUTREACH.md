## 2. Outreach Email Templates

### Template A: Local Artist

**Subject:** Free platform to get more art commissions — Ashtabula-focused

```
Dear [Artist Name],

I came across your work [on Instagram/at the Arts Center/through 
a friend] and wanted to reach out about a new platform designed 
specifically for Ashtabula County artists.

**The challenge for local artists:**
Finding commission work is hard. Etsy and Saatchi take 20-35% 
commission. Instagram has no built-in way to handle commissions. 
Word of mouth is unpredictable.

**Artist Commission Form helps you:**
• Showcase your portfolio to local buyers (and tourists)
• Receive commission requests with all details upfront
• Get pricing guidance so you don't undercharge
• Accept deposits and payments easily
• Manage commission timeline and communication
• Keep 100% of your commission (we charge buyers a small fee)

**How it works:**
1. Create a profile with your portfolio and commission rates
2. Buyers submit requests with budget, timeline, vision
3. You approve/decline with one click
4. Platform handles contracts, deposits, payments
5. You create art and get paid

**Why this is different:**
• Local focus — buyers want Ashtabula artists specifically
• Tourist market — Geneva-on-the-Lake visitors buy local art
• Commission-focused — not just selling existing work
• Professional tools — contracts, payments, communication
• Free for artists — we make money from buyer fees, not your art

**Early access:**
We're looking for 20 local artists to pilot the platform. You'll 
get free premium features for life and input on platform design.

Interested in a 15-minute conversation about your needs?

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Phone]
[Email]
```

## 3. Artist Onboarding Process

### Recruitment Funnel

| Stage | Target | Activity |
|-------|--------|----------|
| **Awareness** | 100 artists | Social media, Arts Center, craft fairs |
| **Interest** | 40 artists | Email Template A, info sessions |
| **Signup** | 20 artists | Profile creation, portfolio upload |
| **Active** | 15 artists | First commission request |
| **Retained** | 12 artists | Ongoing commissions, 6+ months |

### Profile Setup Checklist

**Required:**
- [ ] Artist bio and photo
- [ ] 5-10 portfolio images
- [ ] Commission types offered (portraits, landscapes, etc.)
- [ ] Base pricing (per size, medium, complexity)
- [ ] Availability (lead time, busy seasons)

**Optional but Recommended:**
- [ ] Process description (how commissions work)
- [ ] Testimonials from past clients
- [ ] Instagram/website links
- [ ] Studio location (for local pickup)
- [ ] Shipping capabilities

### Pricing Guidance Tool

**Commission Calculator Inputs:**
- Medium (oil, acrylic, watercolor, digital, etc.)
- Size (small, medium, large, custom)
- Complexity (simple, moderate, detailed)
- Timeline (standard, rush)
- Experience level (emerging, established)

**Example Output:**
"Based on your selections, similar commissions in your area range 
from $350-$550. We recommend starting at $400."

## 5. Competitive Intelligence

### Etsy
- **Commission:** 6.5% + $0.20/listing
- **Focus:** Handmade goods, not commissions
- **Weakness:** Not commission-focused, high fees
- **Our angle:** Commission-specific tools, local focus

### Saatchi Art / Artfinder
- **Commission:** 33-35%
- **Focus:** Gallery sales of existing art
- **Weakness:** High commission, not local
- **Our angle:** Lower fees, Ashtabula-specific

### Instagram
- **Commission:** Free
- **Focus:** Social media
- **Weakness:** No transaction support, disorganized
- **Our angle:** Professional commission management

### Fiverr / Upwork
- **Commission:** 5-20%
- **Focus:** General freelancing
- **Weakness:** Race to bottom, not art-specific
- **Our angle:** Art-specific, quality-focused

**Key Differentiation:**
- **Local focus:** Ashtabula artists only
- **Commission tools:** Contracts, deposits, timeline management
- **Pricing guidance:** Help artists charge fairly
- **Tourist market:** Geneva-on-the-Lake visitor angle
- **Artist-friendly:** Free for artists, buyer pays fees

## 6. Revenue Model Options

### Option 1: Buyer Fee (Recommended for MVP)
- Artists keep 100% of commission
- Buyers pay 10% platform fee
- Example: $500 commission → artist gets $500, buyer pays $550

### Option 2: Subscription (Artist-Paid)
- Free tier: 3 active listings
- Pro tier: $15/month, unlimited commissions
- Premium tier: $30/month, featured placement

### Option 3: Hybrid
- Free: Basic listings, 15% commission
- Pro: $20/month, 5% commission
- Premium: $50/month, 0% commission + featured

    └─► Pause

### Go Criteria
- [ ] 10+ artists commit to creating profiles
- [ ] OR Arts Center agrees to partnership

### No-Go Criteria

- [ ] Artists cite existing platforms as sufficient
- [ ] No differentiation from Etsy/Fiverr perceived
