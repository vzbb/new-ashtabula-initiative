# GOTL Weekend Planner — Pitch Materials

## 30-Second Demo Script

**HOOK:**
"Imagine you're a tourism bureau—and visitors are browsing your website, interested in Geneva-on-the-Lake, but they leave without booking because they can't picture their perfect weekend."

**DEMO (5 steps, ~30 seconds):**

1. *Select "Wine Weekend"* from vibe options
2. *Enter "2"* for number of days
3. *Click "Generate Itinerary"*
4. *AI creates in 10 seconds:* personalized 2-day itinerary with 4-6 activities
5. *Built-in CTA:* direct prompt to book lodging while excitement is high

**WHAT IT DOES:**
AI-powered trip planner that turns curious website browsers into confirmed lodging bookings by generating personalized Geneva-on-the-Lake itineraries in seconds.

**WHO IT'S FOR:**
Visitor bureaus, destination marketing organizations, lodging properties, and tourism boards promoting Geneva-on-the-Lake and Ashtabula County attractions.

**CTA:**
"Let's integrate this into your existing website—most DMOs see 25-40% higher booking conversion within 30 days."

---

## Key Features

1. **Vibe-Based Planning** — Wine Weekend, Family Fun, Romantic Getaway, Adventure, etc.
2. **Flexible Duration** — 1-day trips to extended weekends
3. **AI-Generated Itineraries** — 4-6 curated activities per trip
4. **Integrated Booking CTA** — Drives direct lodging reservations
5. **Local Business Spotlight** — Promotes partner wineries, restaurants, attractions
6. **10-Second Generation** — Instant gratification for planners
7. **Mobile-First Design** — Perfect for on-the-go trip planning
8. **Shareable Output** — Easy to text/email to fellow travelers

---

## Value Proposition

**For Visitors Bureaus:**
- Converts website browsers into bookers
- Reduces planning friction that causes drop-offs
- Showcases diverse local attractions
- Collects trip intent data for marketing

**For Lodging Partners:**
- More qualified leads with trip plans already made
- Higher booking conversion rates
- Longer average stays (multi-day itineraries)
- Reduced cancellation rates (pre-planned trips)

**For Tourists:**
- Eliminates hours of research
- Discovers hidden local gems
- Gets ready-to-use trip plan
- Books with confidence
