## Completed Deliverables

### Phase 1: Research & Recon (2026-02-19)
**File:** `PHASE1-RESEARCH.md` (10.2 KB)

**Key Findings:**
- **Market Gap:** No dominant parking finder for small tourism markets
- **Beachhead Market:** Geneva-on-the-Lake, OH (high tourism, no existing solution)
- **Stakeholders Identified:** GOTL CVB, Geneva Township, Historic Ashtabula Harbor
- **Competitors:** ParkMobile/SpotHero ignore small markets (opportunity)
- **Data Sources:** Municipal partnerships required, no real-time sensors exist

**Strategic Recommendation:**
- Target municipal SaaS model ($200-500/month)
- Start with Geneva-on-the-Lake as pilot ("blue ocean" market)
- Avoid freemium consumer app (low adoption risk in small market)
