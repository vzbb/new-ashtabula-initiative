## 2. Outreach Email Templates

### Template A: ACAPL Initial Outreach (Executive Director)

**Subject:** Partnership Proposal: AI-Powered Pet Matching for Ashtabula County

```
Dear [Executive Director Name],

I'm writing on behalf of a local technology initiative focused on improving pet adoption outcomes in Ashtabula County. We've been researching the challenges facing our local shelters and believe we can help ACAPL reduce return rates while increasing successful adoptions.

THE OPPORTUNITY
We've developed a concept for "Pet-Matchmaker" — a personality-based matching platform specifically for Ashtabula County that would:

• Help adopters find pets whose temperament matches their lifestyle
• Reduce the 10-15% return rate by improving match quality upfront
• Drive more local traffic to ACAPL vs. national platforms
• Provide ACAPL with free tools for personality assessment
• Increase visibility for hard-to-place animals through better matching

HOW IT WORKS
Prospective adopters complete a short lifestyle quiz (home type, activity level, experience). Our AI matches them to available pets at ACAPL based on personality compatibility — not just breed and age. Think "Meet Your Match" but modern, mobile-first, and free for ACAPL.

WHAT WE NEED
We're seeking a pilot partnership with ACAPL to:
1. Understand your current adoption process and pain points
2. Co-design the personality assessment workflow
3. Beta test with 5-10 adoptions over 60 days
4. Measure impact on return rates and adoption speed

WHAT ACAPL GETS
• Free platform access permanently
• Reduced staff time on adoption counseling
• Better-qualified adopters
• Local marketing support
• Data insights on adoption trends

ABOUT US
This is part of the New Ashtabula Initiative, a community-focused project building technology tools to support local businesses and organizations. We're not a startup seeking profit — we're neighbors who want to see ACAPL succeed.

NEXT STEPS
I'd love to schedule a 20-minute call to discuss this opportunity and answer any questions. Would you be available for a brief conversation this week or next?

Thank you for the incredible work ACAPL does for our community's animals. I look forward to the possibility of supporting your mission.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Your Phone]
[Your Email]
```

## 3. Meeting Agenda: ACAPL Discovery Call

### Pre-Meeting Research Checklist
- [ ] Review ACAPL website thoroughly
- [ ] Check their current adoption process online
- [ ] Look at their recent social media posts
- [ ] Note current pet population (if visible online)
- [ ] Research any recent news/articles about ACAPL

### Meeting Agenda (30 Minutes)

**1. Introductions (5 min)**
- Introduce yourself and the New Ashtabula Initiative
- Establish credibility (local focus, community mission)
- Set agenda for the meeting

**2. Discovery Questions (15 min)**

**Current Process:**
- Walk me through your current adoption process from intake to placement
- How do you currently assess animal personalities/temperaments?
- What information do you collect about pets before listing them?

**Challenges:**
- What's your current return rate? Do you track reasons?
- What are the biggest pain points in your adoption process?
- How much staff time is spent on adoption counseling?
- What role do national platforms (Petfinder) play for you?

**Goals:**
- If you could wave a magic wand, what would you change about adoptions?
- What does "successful adoption" look like to you?
- Are there types of animals that are particularly hard to place?

**Technical Capacity:**
- Who would be the primary user of a matching platform on your team?
- What's your comfort level with new technology tools?
- Do you have someone tech-savvy who could champion this internally?

**3. Solution Overview (5 min)**
- Briefly show wireframes/mockups if available
- Explain how personality matching works
- Clarify ACAPL's role in the pilot

**4. Next Steps (5 min)**
- Propose pilot scope (5-10 adoptions, 60 days)
- Agree on timeline
- Schedule follow-up meeting
- Exchange contact information

# Pet-Matchmaker for Ashtabula County
## AI-Powered Personality Matching for Local Pet Adoption

**The Problem:**
• 10-15% of adopted pets are returned due to lifestyle mismatches
• Adopters can't judge personality from photos alone
• Small shelters compete against better-funded urban organizations

**The Solution:**
A free, local matching platform that pairs adopters with pets based on personality compatibility — not just breed and age.

**How It Works:**
1. Adopter completes 2-minute lifestyle quiz
2. AI matches them to compatible pets at ACAPL
3. Adopter views detailed personality profiles
4. Better matches = faster adoptions + fewer returns

**Benefits for ACAPL:**
✓ FREE — no cost, ever
✓ Reduced return rates
✓ Better-qualified adopters
✓ Local marketing support
✓ Data insights on adoption trends

**ABOUT:** This initiative is part of the New Ashtabula Initiative, building technology tools to support our local community. We're neighbors, not a startup seeking profit.
