**Next:** Phase 2 Resource Procurement

## 1. Problem Statement

### The Pain Point
Wedding vendors in Ashtabula County struggle with lead generation:
- **Fragmented marketing:** Vendors must maintain presence on 5+ platforms (The Knot, WeddingWire, Facebook, Instagram, Google)
- **High commission fees:** National platforms take 15-25% of booking value
- **Unqualified leads:** Pay for leads that don't convert (tire kickers, price shoppers)
- **Geographic competition:** Compete with Cleveland/Pittsburgh vendors with bigger budgets
- **No local hub:** No centralized Ashtabula-focused wedding marketplace

### Impact on Couples
- **Overwhelming search:** Must contact vendors individually across multiple platforms
- **No local curation:** Hard to find Ashtabula-specific vendors (Geneva-on-the-Lake venues, local florists)
- **Delayed responses:** Vendors slow to respond to scattered inquiries
- **No package deals:** Can't easily find vendors who work well together

## 4. User Personas

### Primary: "Bride Brittany" (Engaged Couple)
- **Demographics:** 25-35, planning Ashtabula wedding
- **Budget:** $25,000-$35,000
- **Tech comfort:** High
- **Pain points:**
  - Overwhelmed by vendor research across multiple sites
  - Hard to find Geneva-on-the-Lake specific vendors
  - Vendors slow to respond to inquiries
  - No local package deals or vendor recommendations
- **Needs:** One-stop local vendor discovery, quick responses, package options

### Primary: "Vendor Vince" (Wedding Photographer)
- **Demographics:** 30-50, sole proprietor or small team
- **Business:** 15-25 weddings/year
- **Tech comfort:** Moderate
- **Pain points:**
  - The Knot costs $4,000+/year for minimal leads
  - Leads from national platforms often unqualified
  - Competing with Cleveland photographers with bigger budgets
  - Hard to reach Ashtabula couples specifically
- **Needs:** Affordable local leads, qualified inquiries, lower commission

### Secondary: "Planner Patty" (Wedding Planner)
- **Demographics:** 30-45, full-service planner
- **Business:** 20-30 weddings/year
- **Role:** Coordinates all vendors for couples
- **Pain points:**
  - Hard to find reliable local vendors
  - No central database of vendor capabilities/pricing
  - Last-minute vendor substitutions difficult
- **Needs:** Curated vendor network, availability calendar, vendor reviews

### Secondary: "Venue Victor" (Wedding Venue Owner)
- **Demographics:** 40-60, owns winery/B&B venue
- **Business:** 30-50 weddings/year at venue
- **Pain points:**
  - Need to recommend reliable vendors to couples
  - Vendors cancel, need quick replacements
  - Want referral fees from vendor recommendations
- **Needs:** Vendor partner network, referral tracking, commission system

## 5. Solution Concept

### Core Concept
**"The Knot meets Angie's List for Ashtabula County — local wedding vendor discovery with qualified lead generation."**

### Key Features

**For Couples:**
1. **Local Vendor Directory** — All Ashtabula wedding vendors in one place
2. **Smart Matching** — Match by budget, style, date availability
3. **Single Inquiry Form** — Fill once, send to multiple relevant vendors
4. **Vendor Packages** — Pre-curated vendor teams that work well together
5. **Lake Erie Wedding Guide** — Geneva-on-the-Lake specific content
6. **Availability Checker** — See who's free on your date

**For Vendors:**
1. **Lead Dashboard** — Receive and manage qualified leads
2. **Profile Pages** — Photos, reviews, pricing, availability
3. **Pay-Per-Lead** — Only pay for serious inquiries (not subscriptions)
4. **Performance Analytics** — Track conversion rates, ROI
5. **Package Partnerships** — Team with complementary vendors
6. **Referral Network** — Venue and vendor referral commissions

**For Venues:**
1. **Preferred Vendor Lists** — Showcase recommended partners
2. **Referral Tracking** — Track vendor recommendations
3. **Commission System** — Earn from vendor referrals
4. **Couple Matching** — Match couples to venue-appropriate vendors

### Differentiation

| Feature | Wedding Lead Form | The Knot | WeddingWire | Facebook |
|---------|-------------------|----------|-------------|----------|
| Local Ashtabula focus | ✓ | ✗ | ✗ | Partial |
| Pay-per-lead (not subscription) | ✓ | ✗ | ✗ | ✗ |
| Vendor packages | ✓ | ✗ | ✗ | ✗ |
| Geneva-on-the-Lake specialization | ✓ | ✗ | ✗ | ✗ |
| Qualified leads only | ✓ | ✗ | ✗ | ✗ |
| Referral commissions | ✓ | ✗ | ✗ | ✗ |

## 6. Tech Stack Options

### Recommended: Next.js + Supabase + Stripe
- **Frontend:** Next.js 14, Tailwind CSS
- **Database:** Supabase (PostgreSQL)
- **Payments:** Stripe (pay-per-lead transactions)
- **Auth:** Supabase Auth
- **Image Storage:** Supabase Storage (vendor portfolios)
- **Hosting:** Vercel
- **Cost:** ~$50-100/mo (scales with transactions)

### Alternative: Webflow + Airtable
- **Platform:** Webflow (frontend) + Airtable (backend)
- **Forms:** Typeform or native Webflow
- **Cost:** ~$50-80/mo
- **Pros:** Faster to build, easier for non-technical updates
- **Cons:** Limited customization, scaling constraints

## 7. Revenue Model

### Pay-Per-Lead (Primary)
| Lead Type | Price | Description |
|-----------|-------|-------------|
| **Standard lead** | $15-25 | Basic inquiry (date, budget, contact) |
| **Qualified lead** | $35-50 | Pre-screened (venue booked, date confirmed) |
| **Premium lead** | $75-100 | High intent (ready to book, specific date) |

### Subscription Tier (Optional)
| Tier | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Basic** | $0 | Profile only, no leads |
| **Pro** | $49/mo | 5 leads included, priority placement |
| **Business** | $149/mo | 20 leads included, featured listings |

### Referral Commissions
- **Venue-to-vendor referrals:** 5-10% of booking value
- **Vendor-to-vendor referrals:** $25-50 per successful referral
- **Package deals:** 10% of total package value

### Example Economics (Photographer):**
- 10 qualified leads/month @ $35 = $350
- 30% conversion = 3 bookings
- Average booking: $3,000
- Revenue: $9,000
- Lead cost: $350 (3.9% of revenue)
- vs. The Knot: $400/mo + lower quality leads
