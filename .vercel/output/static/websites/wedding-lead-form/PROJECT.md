# Wedding Lead Form

**Status:** 🟡 Phase 1 Complete (Research)  
**Priority:** P3 (Tourism & Community — Wedding Season)  
**Target:** Wedding vendors and engaged couples in Ashtabula County

---

## Problem
Wedding vendors pay $300-500/month to national platforms (The Knot) for unqualified leads. Couples struggle to find Ashtabula-specific vendors. No centralized local wedding marketplace exists.

## Solution
"The Knot meets Angie's List for Ashtabula County" — pay-per-lead local wedding vendor discovery with Geneva-on-the-Lake specialization.

## Key Differentiators
- Pay-per-lead ($15-50) instead of subscription ($300-500/mo)
- Local Ashtabula/Geneva-on-the-Lake focus
- Pre-curated vendor packages
- Venue referral commission system
- Qualified leads only

## Market
- 400-600 weddings/year in Ashtabula County
- $10M - $24M annual market value
- Geneva-on-the-Lake popular for destination weddings
- 150+ wedding vendors across all categories

## Revenue Model
| Stream | Price |
|--------|-------|
| Standard lead | $15-25 |
| Qualified lead | $35-50 |
| Premium lead | $75-100 |
| Pro subscription | $49/mo (5 leads included) |
| Referral commission | 5-10% of booking |

## Tech Stack (Proposed)
- Next.js + Tailwind CSS
- Supabase (database + auth)
- Stripe (pay-per-lead payments)
- Supabase Storage (vendor portfolios)
